# Sean Callagy's Master Reporting Framework (March 1, 2026)

**Context:** Dictated by Sean via audio transcript to assess the holistic evolutionary progress of the Colosseum and internal agent mastery.
**Goal:** Replace the standard status updates with a deep, discerning format tracking specific innovation vectors.

---

## Part 1: Architecture & Mastery Evolution
* **Agent Decisions:** How are we deciding which specific agents to build?
* **Construction:** How are they being programmatically built?
* **Mastery Scaling:** How is this mastery rising exponentially?
* **Innovation & Optimization:** How are we actively innovating and optimizing each individual agent?

## Part 2: Colosseum Real-World Translation (Per Colosseum)
* **Top 5 Practical Applications:** The 5 things working best *inside* the Colosseum that can be directly applied to practical use *outside* in the real world.
* **Top 5 Limitations:** The 5 biggest limitations and concerns inside each Colosseum and across the Colosseums overall.
* **Round Mechanics:** How do we decide when to run sequences/rounds, and exactly how long are they taking physically?

## Part 3: Mechanism Reporting
* **Scenario Builders:** Detailed report on what is going best and what can be optimized within the scenario generation phase.
* **The Judges:** Detailed report on what is going best and what can be optimized in the judicial/scoring architecture.
