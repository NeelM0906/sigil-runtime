## 3. Engagement Examples & Plans (The Micro)
**The SMS Micro-Agreement Protocol**
- We do not cold-spam links via SMS due to TCPA and burn risk. We must earn the right to speak via a Two-Step Micro-Agreement.
- *Example (PI Focus):*
  - *Touch 1 (The Ping):* "Hey [Name], quick question—are you tracking your per-case revenue against the national PI benchmark? Most firms in [City] aren't."
  - *Touch 2 (After Reply):* "Thought so. We built a 2-min diagnostic based on Sean Callagy's systems (two Top 100 verdicts). It pinpoints exactly where firms leak $47K+ per case. Here's your assessment: [Quiz Link]"
