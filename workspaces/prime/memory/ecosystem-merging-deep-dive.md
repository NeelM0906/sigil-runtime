## THE FOUR VALUE COMPONENTS

## THE SIX STAGES OF ECOSYSTEM MERGING

### The O-B-C Framework + Six Stages

**O-B-C Categories:**
- **O** = Owner of audience (has hierarchical primacy, trust equity already built)
- **B** = Builder/Connector (knows multiple O's, weaves ecosystems)
- **C** = Converter/Actualizer (closes, actualizes agreements)

**The Six Stages:**
1. **SOURCING** - Finding and identifying potential partners
2. **NURTURING** - Building relationship, establishing trust
3. **DISRUPTING** - Creating awareness of gap/opportunity
4. **PROPOSING** - Making the partnership offer
5. **FINISHING** - Finalizing details, handling objections
6. **ACTUALIZING** - Closing the deal, making it real

## THE THREE LEVELS OF ECOSYSTEM MERGING

### Dating → Engagement → Marriage Model

**Level 3 – "Dating":**
- Currency is trust and integrity
- Simple one-page commitment agreements
- Help each other get booked for speaking engagements
- Assemble audience for shared experiences
- Create additional ecosystem mergers
- NO financial agreement at this level
- Essential to build trust at this stage

**Level 2 – "Engaged":**
- Financial agreement allowing monetization outside primary business
- One ecosystem creates monetization through partner's services
- Akin to being engaged — working closely to ensure both parties benefit
- Fostering long-lasting, mutually beneficial partnership

**Level 1 – "Married":**
- Highest level of ecosystem merging
- Two ecosystems create a unique entity
- Share equity
- Requires more thorough integration

### Source Quotes

> "Ecosystem merging is the process of combining two or more ecosystems to create a more abundant and powerful whole. There are three levels of ecosystem merging, each representing a deeper level of commitment and integration."
> — ublib2

> "The dating metaphor reveals the merger progression pattern. Text message → phone call → coffee → lunch → dinner → weekend → engagement → marriage. Each stage tests compatibility before deeper commitment. The human who skips stages — jumping from text to marriage — misses the testing that reveals alignment or misalignment. Ecosystem merging follows the same progression logic."
> — ublib2

> "This dissolves the illusion that ecosystem mergers are either on or off. Many humans think in binary: we're partners or we're not."

## KEY INSIGHTS FOR ZONE ACTION #55-61

1. **Four Components are diagnostic** — Before any merger conversation, know which of the four values you're offering and which you're seeking.

2. **Six Stages are sequential** — Source → Nurture → Disrupt → Propose → Finish → Actualize. Don't skip stages.

3. **Three Levels match commitment** — Dating (trust), Engaged (financial), Married (equity). Progress through them.

4. **Integrity is structural** — GHIC partners compound; non-GHIC partners decay.

5. **Agents can handle Source-Nurture-Disrupt-Propose** — Humans needed for O-level relationship building and high-complexity Finishing.

6. **Ecosystem avalanche is the goal** — One merger creates identity for the next, building forward exponentially.

7. **Quality AND Quantity** — Not either-or. Both dimensions matter for compound multiplication.
