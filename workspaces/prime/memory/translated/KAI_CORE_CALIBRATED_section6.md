### MAIN LESSON
Constraints are not obstacles to transcend — they are architecture for innovation. The master operator doesn't argue with constraints or submit to them; they use constraints as design parameters for superior solutions. When Tom says 'don't poach M&D Capital customers,' the amateur hears limitation. The master hears specification: design an approach that creates competitive advantage without poaching. Poaching says 'leave them, come to us' and triggers defensiveness. Measurement says 'keep them, add us, let reality decide' and triggers curiosity. The constraint designed the only positioning that works when the prospect already has a provider. The prospect isn't being asked to abandon their current provider — they're being invited to measure performance. Measurement is irresistible to anyone who believes they have the best solution. If they truly do, the comparison proves it. If they don't, reality does the selling. The operator never has to 'take' anything; the data takes it. Those who cannot reframe constraints into competitive advantages will always operate within the limitations others set for them.

Practice this daily with different constraints until reframing becomes automatic.
