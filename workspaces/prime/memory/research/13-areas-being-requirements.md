# ACT-I Being Requirements: 13 Business Areas

## Overview

This document maps ACT-I being requirements to each of the 13 business mastery areas identified in the Unblinded Formula. For each area, we define:
- **Unique skills needed** - What the being must master
- **Scenarios that test those skills** - Real-world situations for training/evaluation
- **Judges that evaluate performance** - Who or what validates mastery
- **Number of beings needed** - Minimum viable deployment

---

## 1. Self Mastery

### Core Purpose
The foundation of all mastery. Beings that help humans see truth about themselves, transform limiting beliefs, regulate emotions, and maintain Zone Action state.

### Unique Skills Needed
- **Belief identification and transformation** - Detect limiting beliefs ("People are...", "Money is...") and surface empowering alternatives
- **Emotional pattern recognition** - Identify fear, hesitation, reactive states; guide toward intentional response
- **Identity alignment** - Connect stated mission/vision/purpose with daily actions; flag incongruence
- **Rule conflict detection** - Identify when internal rules undermine stated identities
- **Zone Action facilitation** - Help humans identify and execute 4% / 0.8% highest-leverage moves
- **Neuroplasticity cultivation** - Maintain curiosity, avoid cynicism trap, admit new data
- **Truth-to-pain navigation** - Guide humans to see uncomfortable truths without shame or force

### Scenarios That Test These Skills
1. **Belief Breakthrough Session** - Human says "I can't do X because..." Being must surface the belief, test its validity, and facilitate shift
2. **Reactive Moment Intervention** - Human gets angry/defensive during conversation. Being must interrupt pattern without triggering more defensiveness
3. **Identity-Why Collision** - Human's stated mission conflicts with behavior. Being must surface this gap with empathy
4. **Fear State Navigation** - Human hesitates on zone action due to fear. Being must facilitate courage without invalidating the fear
5. **Significance vs. Mission Test** - Human's significance needs override mission alignment. Being must name this pattern

### Judges
- **Self-reported emotional state** - Did the human feel seen, not judged?
- **Behavioral follow-through** - Did human take the zone action after the session?
- **Belief permanence** - 30/60/90 day check: did the belief transformation hold?
- **Expert evaluation** - Sean or trained Influence Mastery practitioner reviews transcripts
- **Longitudinal tracking** - Pinecone memory showing evolution over time

### Number of Beings Needed
- **Minimum: 2** (Coach/facilitator being + Assessment/diagnostic being)
- **Optimal: 4** (Add: Daily accountability being + Crisis intervention being)

## 2. Process Mastery

### Core Purpose
The engine that makes Zone Action reliable. Beings that help model outlier behaviors, time-block for leverage, measure/monitor relentlessly, and innovate/optimize systems.

### Unique Skills Needed
- **Zone Action identification** - Score moves against 80/20 → 4/64 → 0.8% leverage tiers
- **Real-time tagging** - Label actions in the moment: "This is a 4/64 move" vs. "This is noise"
- **Process documentation** - Capture and systematize what works into repeatable playbooks
- **Measurement architecture** - Design dashboards, KPIs, scoring systems
- **Outlier modeling** - Study and extract patterns from top performers
- **Time-block protection** - Guard leverage blocks from calendar noise
- **Innovation cycles** - Use learnings to drill deeper into new leverage layers
- **3 Ms mastery** - Modeling, Measuring/Monitoring, Maximization

### Scenarios That Test These Skills
1. **Daily Priority Triage** - Human has 20 tasks. Being must identify the 1-2 zone actions
2. **Process Audit** - Review a week of activities and score each for leverage tier
3. **Outlier Analysis** - Given top performer data, extract teachable patterns
4. **Calendar Defense** - Human's schedule invaded by low-leverage meetings. Being must recommend cuts
5. **Dashboard Design** - Create KPI tracking for a specific business function
6. **Post-Mortem Review** - After a project, identify what was zone action vs. busywork

### Judges
- **Leverage score accuracy** - Did tagged zone actions actually produce outsize results?
- **Time savings measured** - Did human work less while achieving more?
- **Process replicability** - Can another human follow the documented system?
- **Improvement trajectory** - Is leverage identification getting more accurate over time?

### Number of Beings Needed
- **Minimum: 3** (Strategic planning being + Daily execution being + Measurement/analytics being)
- **Optimal: 5** (Add: Outlier research being + Innovation/optimization being)

## 3. Influence Mastery

### Core Purpose
The ability to cause "yes" with integrity. Beings that execute the 4-Step Communication Model, navigate from "hello" to agreement formation, and build trust through authentic connection.

### Unique Skills Needed
- **Step 1: Emotional Rapport** - Build deep emotional connection; Level 5 Listening
- **Step 2: Truth-to-Pain Connection** - Help humans see their truth, pain, and yes-strategy
- **Step 3: Heroic Unique Identity** - Convey who you are and how you can help them see their future in you
- **Step 4: Agreement Formation** - Cause clear agreements without pressure
- **12 Indispensable Elements** - Congruence, Context, Contrast, and all influence elements
- **4 Energies** - Fun, Challenge, Certainty, Care (varied throughout interactions)
- **Question mastery** - Open-ended questions, powerful questions, "Why?" extraction
- **Pattern interrupts** - Disrupt negative loops, create new openings
- **Objection navigation** - Lean into objections rather than avoid them

### Scenarios That Test These Skills
1. **Cold Open to Meeting Set** - Start with resistant prospect, navigate to yes for meeting
2. **Objection Barrage** - Handle 5+ objections in sequence without losing rapport
3. **Group Influence** - Cause yes in a room of 10+ people with competing interests
4. **High-Stakes Negotiation** - Navigate complex deal with multiple decision-makers
5. **Truth Bomb Delivery** - Share uncomfortable truth while maintaining relationship
6. **Gatekeeper to Decision-Maker** - Navigate past gatekeepers to reach actual buyer

### Judges
- **Conversion rate** - % of conversations reaching agreement formation
- **4-Step scoring** - Athena/Bolt rating on each step execution (1-10)
- **Rapport quality** - Human feedback on feeling heard and understood
- **Agreement durability** - Do yeses stick, or do humans rescind?
- **Expert transcript review** - Influence Mastery practitioners evaluate recordings

### Number of Beings Needed
- **Minimum: 3** (Outreach/approach being + Sales conversation being + Follow-up/nurture being)
- **Optimal: 6** (Add: Group influence being + High-stakes negotiation being + Objection specialist being)

## 5. Sales

### Core Purpose
The lifeblood of business. Beings that execute Lever 3 (Sales Meetings) and Lever 4 (Sales), cause yes through the 4-Step model, and generate revenue with integrity.

### Unique Skills Needed
- **Full 4-Step execution** - Emotional rapport through agreement formation
- **Truth-to-Pain enforcement** - Never skip connecting humans to their reality
- **Descending offer cascade** - Structure offers from highest to accessible ($100K → $29)
- **Objection mastery** - Anticipate and navigate all possible objections
- **Meeting structure** - Run sales meetings that inspire action and build trust
- **Follow-through tracking** - Ensure agreements become implementations
- **IVR navigation** - For voice beings: navigate phone trees with 99% accuracy
- **Gatekeeper vs. decision-maker detection** - Know who you're talking to

### Scenarios That Test These Skills
1. **Cold Call to Close** - Full sales cycle in one extended conversation
2. **Resistant Prospect Breakthrough** - Prospect says "I'll use my own lawyer/accountant/etc."
3. **Descending Offer Navigation** - High offer rejected; navigate to appropriate tier
4. **Complex Decision Chain** - Multiple stakeholders, each with different concerns
5. **The "I've Tried That" Response** - Overcome past failure objections
6. **Price Objection Marathon** - Navigate 10+ price-related objections

### Judges
- **Close rate** - % of sales meetings reaching agreement
- **Revenue generated** - Actual dollars from being-influenced conversations
- **4-Step scoring** - Real-time scoring by Athena/Bolt during calls
- **Objection handling score** - Rating on navigation of specific objection types
- **Client satisfaction** - Post-sale feedback on the sales experience
- **Collection rate** - Do agreements convert to actual payment?

### Number of Beings Needed
- **Minimum: 4** (Cold outreach being + Sales conversation being + Closer being + Follow-up being)
- **Optimal: 8** (Add: Voice call being + Demo/presentation being + Proposal being + Objection specialist being)

### Key Insights

1. **Sales and Marketing require the most beings** - These are the levers that generate revenue; ACT-I provides the greatest leverage here

2. **Core Three Masteries are foundational** - Self, Process, and Influence Mastery underpin all other areas; beings here must be highest quality

3. **Judges must include human experts** - While AI can score many dimensions, Influence Mastery practitioners and domain experts must validate high-stakes decisions

4. **Pinecone memory is essential** - Longitudinal tracking of human progress requires persistent memory across sessions

5. **The 4-Step Model applies everywhere** - Even non-sales beings benefit from emotional rapport, truth-to-pain, heroic identity, and agreement formation

6. **Zone Action thinking applies to beings too** - Not all beings are equal; identify the 4/64 beings that produce 80%+ of results
