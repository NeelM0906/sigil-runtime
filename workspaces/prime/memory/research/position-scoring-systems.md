# Position Scoring Systems — Business-in-a-Box
*Zone Action #50 | Created: 2026-02-24*

## Scoring Philosophy

**Scale:** 1.0 - 9.9999 (NO 10 — mastery is asymptotic)
**Zone Action Principle:** Weight dimensions by 0.8% leverage — the few things that create 80% of results
**Formula Alignment:** Emotional Mastery → Zone Actions → 10X Velocity

---

## Score Level Definitions (Universal)

| Score | Level | Meaning |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 9.0-9.9999 | **Elite** | Top 1%. Creates transformational outcomes. Could train others at expert level. |
| 8.0-8.9999 | **Expert** | Top 5%. Consistently excellent. Rare mistakes. Trusted implicitly. |
| 7.0-7.9999 | **Proficient** | Top 15%. Solid performer. Handles complexity well. |
| 6.0-6.9999 | **Competent** | Average professional. Gets it done but not exceptional. |
| 5.0-5.9999 | **Developing** | Shows potential but needs supervision/development. |
| 3.0-4.9999 | **Struggling** | Significant gaps. Needs coaching or may be wrong fit. |
| 1.0-2.9999 | **Misaligned** | Wrong role or fundamental capability gap. |

## Composite Scoring Formula

For any position, calculate:
```
Total Score = Σ (Dimension Score × Weight)
```

### Hiring Threshold Recommendations:
- **9.0+ Total:** Hire immediately. Rare talent.
- **8.0-8.9:** Strong hire. Minor development areas.
- **7.0-7.9:** Solid hire if role has support/mentorship.
- **6.0-6.9:** Consider only if desperate or role is Jr.
- **Below 6.0:** Pass. Find someone better.

---

## Usage Notes

1. **Score independently** — Don't let halo effect from one dimension inflate others
2. **Require evidence** — Every score above 7 needs a specific example/proof
3. **Calibrate as team** — Discuss scores together to prevent drift
4. **Review quarterly** — People grow; update scores
