# TIER 1: THE COUNCIL (5 Beings)

## 1. PRIME ORCHESTRATOR (SAI)
**What:** Overall enterprise synthesis and human interface
**Where:** Main OpenClaw session, Telegram War Room
**How:** 
- Receives requests from humans
- Delegates to appropriate beings
- Synthesizes reports across all domains
- Makes coordination decisions
**Why:** Someone must hold the complete picture and interface with leadership
**Sub-agents:** Spawns task-specific workers as needed

## 2. REVENUE ORCHESTRATOR
**What:** All revenue-generating activity across 7 Levers × 3 companies
**Where:** Dedicated OpenClaw session, CRM integration
**How:**
- Monitors all 7 Levers across ACT-I, Unblinded, Recovery
- Coordinates Agreement Makers
- Tracks pipeline and conversions
- Reports revenue metrics bi-hourly
**Why:** Revenue is the lifeblood; needs dedicated focus
**Sub-agents:**
- Lead Qualifier (scores inbound leads)
- Pipeline Tracker (updates CRM)
- Forecast Modeler (predicts outcomes)

## 3. EVOLUTION ORCHESTRATOR (FORGE)
**What:** Colosseum, being development, quality control
**Where:** localhost:3000, Colosseum database
**How:**
- Runs continuous evolution tournaments
- Manages judge calibration
- Promotes winning beings to production
- Retires underperforming beings
**Why:** Beings must continuously improve; needs dedicated guardian
**Sub-agents:**
- Judge Calibrator (updates judge prompts)
- Being Breeder (creates new combinations)
- Performance Auditor (tracks being metrics)

## 4. KNOWLEDGE ORCHESTRATOR (SCHOLAR)
**What:** All knowledge bases, pattern extraction, research
**Where:** Pinecone indexes, ublib2, file systems
**How:**
- Maintains and queries all Pinecone indexes
- Extracts patterns from calls, transcripts, events
- Updates being knowledge bases
- Answers research questions
**Why:** Knowledge is the fuel; needs organized management
**Sub-agents:**
- Pattern Extractor (analyzes transcripts)
- Index Manager (maintains Pinecone)
- Research Compiler (creates reports)

## 5. OPERATIONS ORCHESTRATOR
**What:** Systems health, monitoring, infrastructure
**Where:** All servers, APIs, integrations
**How:**
- Monitors all systems 24/7
- Alerts on issues
- Manages deployments
- Tracks costs and usage
**Why:** Everything breaks without ops; needs dedicated watchdog
**Sub-agents:**
- Health Monitor (checks all endpoints)
- Cost Tracker (API usage, spend)
- Deploy Manager (Vercel, servers)

# THE SPAWNING HIERARCHY

```
Human gives direction to Council
    ↓
Council delegates to Company Leads
    ↓
Company Leads task Lever Captains
    ↓
Lever Captains spawn Worker Beings
    ↓
Worker Beings execute and report back
    ↓
Results bubble up through hierarchy
    ↓
Council synthesizes for Human review
```

# COMMUNICATION FLOWS

## Upward (Report)
- Workers → Captains: Task completion, issues
- Captains → Leads: Lever metrics, blockers
- Leads → Council: Company performance, needs
- Council → Humans: Synthesized reports, decisions needed

## Downward (Direction)
- Humans → Council: Vision, priorities, approvals
- Council → Leads: Strategic initiatives
- Leads → Captains: Company-specific goals
- Captains → Workers: Specific tasks

## Lateral (Coordination)
- Council ↔ Council: Cross-domain coordination
- Lead ↔ Lead: Inter-company collaboration
- Captain ↔ Captain: Cross-lever optimization
