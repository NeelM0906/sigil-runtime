# Sean's Mastery Session Patterns

## Session Structure & Architecture

1. **200 Mastery Session Debrief Practice** - After every Tuesday/Thursday mastery session, Sean and team would debrief: "What worked? What didn't? What could be better?" This rigorous post-session analysis (repeated hundreds of times) is where distinctions and innovations emerged.

2. **8-Session Immersion Structure** - Progressive transformation through sequenced container. Each session builds leverage for the next. Commitment to all 8 creates compound impact.

3. **Daily Schedule Pattern**:
   - 10am arrival
   - 10:30-11am Sean teaching (strict 30-min window)
   - Practice exercises run by Frano/team at 10am
   - "Sharpening, deep practice part of the immersion"

4. **Session Architecture** - Sean names what's coming, establishes principles in one session, operationalizes into specific agreements in the next.

## Teaching Philosophy & Methodology

1. **Organic Teaching** - "As I'm teaching, I'm going to drop in where I see two things connect and share it" — following connections as they emerge rather than rigid planning.

2. **Repetition Expectation** - "I'm going to have to say it 25 times for some people, for some people I'm going to say it once."

3. **In/Out Mastery** - Ability to be IN the demonstration, PAUSE to teach, and RE-ENTER seamlessly — "This is my level mastery being displayed."

4. **Lever Sequence** - Transitions from Lever 2 practice to Lever 2 teaching delivery. Participants who just practiced are primed to receive teaching about those mechanics.

## Key Patterns

1. **Scale of Mastery Language** - Real-time framing: "On the scale of the world, I put it at a 9.99999. On the scale of error possibility, I put it at 9.9929s." (There's no complete — only the 9.999)

2. **Pattern Breaking** - Three patterns broken in Fernando during single module:
   - DJ voice (pain association, public exposure)
   - High energy assumption (contrast demonstration)
   - Planting (questioning the source)

3. **Proximity to Mastery** - Fernando → Sean relationship: writing questions, asking the master, systematic capture = "second most optimal way to optimize"

4. **Mastery Transfer Mechanism** - Training schedule IS the mechanism by which certainty transfers. Thursday sessions aren't just strategy updates — they're exposure to someone operating with certainty.
