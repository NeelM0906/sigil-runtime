| **Challenger Sale** | Teaching for Control | Teach, Tailor, Take Control |
| **MEDDIC/MEDDPICC** | Enterprise Qualification | Metrics, Economic Buyer, Decision Criteria, etc. |

### The 4-Step Model (Sean Callagy)
1. **Step 1: Rapport & Connection** — Establish genuine human connection
2. **Step 2: Gap Discovery** — Identify the gap between current state and desired state
3. **Step 3: Heroic Identity** — Share your story with integrity; let them see their future in you
4. **Step 4: Offer & Agreement** — Bridge the gap with your solution

### Key Principles
- "Nothing has ever been created between two humans without a 'yes'"
- Influence with absolute integrity
- Create gravitational pull through heroic storytelling
- Gap = Pain that motivates action (not cheerleading)
- "Everything begins after 'yes'"

## 7. FINANCE

### Gold Standard Models
| Expert | Focus | Key Contribution |
|--------|-------|------------------|
| **Warren Buffett** ⭐ | Value Investing | Intrinsic value, moat, long-term thinking |
| **Charlie Munger** | Mental Models | Latticework of knowledge, inversion |
| **Aswath Damodaran** | Valuation | "Dean of Valuation", DCF mastery |
| **John Malone** | Capital Allocation | Tax-efficient structures, TCI/Liberty |
| **Ray Dalio** | Macro/Principles | Bridgewater, "Principles", economic machine |
| **Peter Lynch** | Growth Investing | "One Up on Wall Street", invest in what you know |

### Key Frameworks
- **Economic Value Added (EVA)** — NOPAT - (Capital × WACC)
- **Discounted Cash Flow (DCF)** — Present value of future cash flows
- **DuPont Analysis** — ROE decomposition (Margin × Turnover × Leverage)
- **Unit Economics** — CAC, LTV, LTV:CAC ratio, payback period
- **Zero-Based Budgeting** — Justify all expenses from zero
- **Cash Conversion Cycle** — Days to convert inventory to cash

### Buffett's Key Principles
1. **Circle of Competence** — Know what you know
2. **Margin of Safety** — Buy below intrinsic value
3. **Economic Moat** — Sustainable competitive advantage
4. **Owner's Earnings** — Net income + D&A - maintenance CapEx
5. **Float** — Use of OPM (other people's money) for investment

### Essential Books
1. "The Intelligent Investor" — Benjamin Graham
2. "Poor Charlie's Almanack" — Charlie Munger
3. "The Essays of Warren Buffett" — Lawrence Cunningham (ed.)
4. "Principles" — Ray Dalio
