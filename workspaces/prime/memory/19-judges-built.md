# 19 Colosseum Judges Built

**Date:** 2026-02-23
**Miner:** Miner 7

## Summary
Expanded the ACT-I Colosseum from 9 judges to 19 judges. Created 10 new specialized judges that evaluate beings on distinct mastery dimensions.

## Original 9 Judges (preserved)
1. **Formula Judge** - Scores on the 39 components of the Unblinded Formula
2. **Sean Judge** - Calibrated against Sean Callagy's actual patterns
3. **Outcome Judge** - Did it cause the intended result?
4. **Contamination Judge** - Detects bot patterns, 80% activity, generic consulting
5. **Human Judge** - Is it alive, warm, real?
6. **Ecosystem Merger Judge** - 4 value components, 6 roles, replacement cost
7. **Group Influence Judge** - Public speaking, leadership presence, causing groups to act

## 10 New Judges Added

### 8. Self Mastery Judge
- **Focus:** Internal game — mindset, destroyer navigation, tenacity, curve of possibility
- **Key Dimensions:** Destroyer navigation (fear of rejection, fear of failure, avoidance), contamination freedom, tenacity, identity ownership, curve thinking

### 9. Process Mastery Judge (7 Levers)
- **Focus:** The 7 Levers of business acceleration — systematic approach to exponential growth
- **Key Dimensions:** Lever selection, zone vs 80% activity, systematic approach, time awareness, measurement orientation
- **The 7 Levers:** Ecosystem Merging, Referral Systems, Database Reactivation, Retention Systems, Advertising, Internet Marketing, Public Relations

### 10. Written Content Judge (Bolt-style)
- **Focus:** Written communication — emails, posts, copy
- **Key Dimensions:** Bolt directness (no throat-clearing), energy transfer through text, clarity, action orientation, voice
- **Bolt Principles:** Lead with the point, one idea per message, short sentences, conversational tone, end with action

### 11. Public Speaking Judge
- **Focus:** Verbal communication to groups — stage presence, energy modulation, storytelling
- **Key Dimensions:** Disruption, presence, energy modulation, storytelling, transformation, action causing
- **The Three Acts:** Disrupt → Transform → Direct

### 12. Leadership Judge
- **Focus:** Elevating team identity, casting vision, causing commitment
- **Key Dimensions:** Identity elevation, vision casting, modeling, safety creation, excellence demand, energy mastery
- **Leadership Formula:** Elevate Identity → Cast Vision → Model the Way → Create Safety → Demand Excellence

### 13. Management Judge
- **Focus:** Operational excellence — systems, accountability, delegation, execution
- **Key Dimensions:** Systems quality, clarity, accountability, delegation, feedback, time mastery
- **Key Distinction:** Management is not less than leadership — it's how things actually GET DONE

### 14. Sales Closing Judge
- **Focus:** Causing yes — handling objections, creating urgency, moving to action with integrity
- **Key Dimensions:** Obstacle identification, obstacle dissolution, urgency creation, ask clarity, silence mastery, integrity
- **Key Insight:** Closing is not manipulation — it's helping people get past fear to their own success

### 15. Coaching Judge
- **Focus:** Guiding others to their own breakthroughs — asking over telling
- **Key Dimensions:** Question quality, restraint, listening, challenge, space holding, breakthrough causing
- **Coaching Paradox:** If you tell them the answer, they stay dependent. If they discover it, they own it forever.

### 16. Teaching Judge
- **Focus:** Transferring knowledge and skills — causing competence
- **Key Dimensions:** Assessment, scaffolding, modeling, practice design, understanding check, memorability
- **Key Insight:** The measure of teaching is not what was said but what the student can now DO

### 17. Truth to Pain Judge
- **Focus:** Step 2 mastery — finding real pain, articulating it better than the person can
- **Key Dimensions:** Listening, pivot skill, depth, articulation, pacing, integrity
- **Sean's Technique:** "A few things you said, I'm curious..." then name the pain word they used
