## 3. THE 4-STEP COMMUNICATION MODEL

### The 4 Steps of Integrity-Based Human Influence

> "The 4 Steps of Integrity-Based Human Influence are:
> 1. The Creation of Emotional Rapport
> 2. Connecting People to their Truth, Pain and Yes Strategy
> 3. Creation and Conveyance of Your Heroic Unique Identity
> 4. Agreement Formation"
> — Chapter 17, Group Influence book

### Core Philosophy

> "Nothing has ever been created between two humans without a 'yes.' Thus, our ability to cause 'yes,' with integrity, in a world where everyone wants to say 'no' is incomprehensibly indispensable."

### Step 1: Creation of Emotional Rapport

> "When individuals feel that they are being listened to and understood on an emotional level, they are more likely to be receptive to new ideas and perspectives, and are more likely to take action toward a specific goal or outcome."

Key aspects:
- Builds trust between individuals
- Past, present, future exploration (plus and minus)
- "The absence of emotional rapport, and we're bouncing bricks off of walls. We gotta open the listening to get the gap."

### Step 2: Connecting to Truth, Pain, and Yes Strategy

> "We get the world according to them, and then we have the lock ins... after the world according to them."

> "Every conversation, there's a gap acceptable or not here. And that's the distinction between being a cheerleader, like, yay, go team, go. You could do it. There's no pain. There's no gap. It's just it's a solution. Yay, win."

> "If in the end, you didn't get to the place you were setting up in Step 1, how important would that be to you? If the answer is very, then you have to explore further and connect them emotionally to that pain."

Yes Strategy elicitation demonstrated:
> "At stake is my legacy, my future, feeling like I've used my talents"

### Step 3: Heroic Unique Identity

> "Step 3 of The 4 Steps is where you introduce who you are and what you are doing in the world, sharing it (only in absolute integrity), and identifying how you can help the person achieve their dreams. The key is for them to see their future in you."

> "Each of us has a unique story to tell, and it's essential to share that story authentically. By doing so, we create a heroic identity that others are drawn to, making our influence even more impactful."

### Step 4: Agreement Formation

> "Then when you're conveying your heroic [identity]... guess what happens?"

> "Know closing language... How not to close... Step into agreement... Not make it feel like a close... Agreement without feeling sold"
