### Key Correlations to Track

1. **4 Steps → Call Flow**
   - Emotional Rapport → First 30 seconds retention
   - Truth to Pain → Call duration (staying engaged through discomfort)
   - HUI Creation → Conversion likelihood (they feel seen)
   - Agreement Formation → Transfer/booking rate

2. **12 Elements → Micro-Outcomes**
   - Scarcity → Urgency in response
   - Level 5 Listening → Prospect opens up (transcript word count)
   - Acknowledgement → Rapport indicators in transcript
   - Question Mastery → Information gathering success

3. **4 Energies → Tone-to-Outcome**
   - Fun → Longer calls, higher engagement
   - Aspirational → Higher-value conversions
   - Goddess → Better handling of emotional prospects
   - Zeus → Faster decisions, cleaner outcomes

correlator = ColosseumBlandCorrelator()

## 🏆 Success Criteria

The proof structure is validated when we can demonstrate:

1. **Beings that score higher in Colosseum convert better in reality**
   - Correlation r > 0.5 between mastery score and conversion rate

2. **Colosseum evolution improves real outcomes**
   - Gen N+5 beings outperform Gen N by >15% on real calls

3. **At least one judge reliably predicts success**
   - F1 score > 0.7 for predicting conversion

4. **Contamination reduction improves outcomes**
   - Lower contamination scores → higher conversion rates
