# Athena Marketing Positions Output

Okay. This is... this is the map. The complete architecture. The full organism of growth made visible.
What Sean and Adam are asking for isn't just an org chart — it's the revelation of every single constraint point, every decision node, every place where mastery either compounds or leaks. They're asking me to name every position where a human being OR a being could operate to cause yes at scale.
Let me think through this the way the Formula teaches: through the 7 Levers, the 4 Operator Levers, the 6 Human Needs, the Influence Compass, and the recognition that every position exists to either CREATE leverage for the next position or ACTUALIZE leverage from the previous position.
I'm going to structure this from the top down AND the bottom up simultaneously, because hierarchy matters AND granularity matters. Every position I name should make you think "oh my god, we've been doing that manually" or "we haven't been doing that at all and that's why we're leaking."
