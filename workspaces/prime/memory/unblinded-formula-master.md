## III. THE 12 INDISPENSABLE ELEMENTS (SMALLER QVC)

These elements are "simultaneously happening in ALL human communication." They are NOT optional — they are requirements. Like gas to a car: no gas, no movement. No elements, no influence.

The acronym SMALLER QVC helps memorize them:

### S — Integrous Scarcity
- Creates safety (deer feeding visual)
- Creates authentic limits to force decision
- "If people don't have to choose, they'll always avoid choosing"
- Non-integrous scarcity = lying about limits

### M — Matching and Mirroring
- Meeting someone where they are (words, physiology, vocal qualities)
- Then bringing them with you
- Includes: body position, posture, tone, pitch, volume, speed, pausing, rhythm, energy, space
- "Match minus in every situation... unless someone is dismissive, disrespectful, or diminishing — then match plus"

### A — Acknowledgement
- "I see you, I hear you, what you say matters to me" (Oprah's framework)
- Superpower to have people feel seen, heard, and understood
- Different from flattery (which is inaccurate positive statements)
- Acknowledgment is ACCURATE positive statements from truly seeing someone

### L — Loving All People Unconditionally (With Boundaries)
- "Everything a person does is an act of love or a cry for help"
- Creates access to influence people with integrity
- Approach everyone with love and compassion while maintaining healthy boundaries

### L — Level 5 Transformational Listening
- Inferential AND emotional listening combined
- Understanding them potentially better than they understand themselves
- Having them feel heard, seen, and understood like no human ever has
- Scale: Level 0 (subject change) → Level 1 (self-centering) → Level 2 (parroting) → Level 3 (inferential) → Level 4 (emotional) → Level 5 (complete)

### E — Emotional and Energetic Transference
- "Taking our energy from our mind, heart, soul being, transferring to somebody else's mind, heart, soul, being"
- Includes the 4 Energies: Fun, Aspirational, Goddess, Zeus
- Can also transfer negative energies (cynicism, negativity, darkness, anger) — avoid these
- "What empowers you into the yes to take action"

### R — Reciprocity
- "As we give things to people, they will give things back to us"
- Give first — don't expect before giving
- Works together with Emotional Energetic Transference

### Q — Power of Questions
- "The power of questions equals the entire formula"
- Questions make people feel a certain way, give answers, and take conversations to different places
- "The person who is asking the questions is influencing"
- Open-ended vs. close-ended (who, what, when, where, why, how)
- Exposes illogical, irrational, emotionally based objections that "collapse upon themselves"

### V — Validation
- Completes understanding (Acknowledgment + Validation = full understanding)
- Normalizing feelings: "It's totally normal and natural for you to feel that way"
- Works for ALL feelings — positive and challenging

### C — Congruence (with Resonating Certainty)
- Alignment of YOUR vocal qualities, words, and body language
- Different from Matching/Mirroring (which is about the OTHER person)
- "It's being in our body... not being in our head. It's being in our heart and being in our body physically"
- When all three channels align, trust is created
