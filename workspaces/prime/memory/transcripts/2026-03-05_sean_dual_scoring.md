## WHAT THIS MEANS FOR IMPLEMENTATION
Every being gets TWO scores:
1. **Formula Score** — How well does this being operate the Unblinded Formula? (39 components, creature scale)
2. **Technical Score** — How masterful is this being at its specific craft? (domain-defined criteria)

**Net mastery = weakest of the two.** A being with 9.9 Formula score and 3.0 Technical score is operating at 3.0. The Formula without craft is philosophy. Craft without the Formula is mechanics.

The Formula Judge is UNIVERSAL — same judge, same 39 components, every Colosseum.
The Technical Judge is SPECIALIZED — different criteria per cluster, defined by the beings themselves.
