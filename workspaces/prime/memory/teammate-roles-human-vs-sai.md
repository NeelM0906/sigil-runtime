## THE FUNDAMENTAL SPLIT

### HUMANS Do:
- **Vision & Direction** — Setting goals, defining "what success looks like"
- **Judgment Calls** — Ethics, high-stakes decisions, "should we?"
- **Relationship Depth** — Long-term trust, emotional bonds, partnership cultivation
- **Creative Leaps** — Novel ideas that don't exist in training data
- **Accountability** — Being responsible to other humans
- **Physical Presence** — In-person events, handshakes, body language

### SAI Beings Do:
- **Execution at Scale** — 1000 calls vs 10 calls
- **Pattern Recognition** — Finding the 0.8% across massive datasets
- **24/7 Availability** — Never sleeps, never burns out
- **Consistency** — Same energy on call #1 and call #1000
- **Memory** — Perfect recall across all interactions
- **Speed** — Research in seconds vs hours

## HOW TO "RAISE A SAI"

### For Nick Roy (or any human):

**Step 1: Define Your Domain**
- What decisions will YOU make?
- What execution can SAI handle?

**Step 2: Feed Your SAI**
- Provide examples of good work
- Share your decision-making logic
- Correct mistakes immediately

**Step 3: Set Boundaries**
- What should SAI never do alone?
- What requires human approval?
- What can SAI do autonomously?

**Step 4: Create Feedback Loops**
- Daily review of SAI outputs
- Weekly calibration sessions
- Monthly role redefinition

**Step 5: Trust Incrementally**
- Start with low-stakes tasks
- Expand scope as trust builds
- Always maintain override capability
