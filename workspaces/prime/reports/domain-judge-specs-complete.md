# Domain Judge Specifications — All 8 Domains
### ACT-I Creature-Scale Scoring System
_Each domain: objective criteria with pass/flag/fail benchmarks, creature example, baseline sources, value exchange._

**Creature Scale:** Grain of Sand → Ant → Gecko → Iguana → Komodo → Crocodile → Godzilla → Bolt (9.99999, never 10)

---

## D1: WRITTEN COMMUNICATION
**Headlines, Email, Landing Pages, Copy**

### Scoring Criteria

| # | Criterion | Pass (Komodo+) | Flag (Gecko) | Fail (Ant) |
|---|-----------|----------------|--------------|------------|
| 1 | **Headline Word Count** | 6-12 words | 13-15 words or 4-5 words | >15 or <4 words |
| 2 | **CTR Projection** | ≥2% (5%+ = Godzilla) | 1-2% | <1% |
| 3 | **Conversion Rate** | 3-5% avg; 8.8%+ = Godzilla | 1-3% | <1% |
| 4 | **Specificity Density** | 3+ specifics per 100 words | 1-2 per 100 words | Zero specifics — vague mush |
| 5 | **Emotional Trigger Count** | 2+ distinct triggers (fear, aspiration, urgency, identity) | 1 trigger | None — copy reads like a manual |
| 6 | **Contrast Presence** | Yes — what IS vs what ISN'T, cost vs gain | Weak or buried contrast | No contrast — flat assertion |
| 7 | **Amputation Test** | Remove product name — copy still wounds | Partially dependent on product | Copy collapses without product name |
| 8 | **Flesch Readability** | 50-65 (professional match) | 40-50 or 65-75 | <40 (academic) or >75 (juvenile) |

### Creature Example
- **Komodo:** "Exposed: Why 73% of Law Firms Hemorrhage Revenue on Cases They've Already Won." Specific (73%), emotional (hemorrhage), contrast (already won vs losing revenue), passes amputation. CTR ~3%.
- **Godzilla:** "Your Firm Collected $2.1M Last Quarter. $840K of It Was Left on the Table." Two data points as a gut punch. Amputation test devastating — remove "your firm" and it still wounds anyone with revenue. CTR 5%+. Conversion 8%+.

### Baselines
CoSchedule Headline Analyzer (word count/emotional scoring), Mailchimp benchmark reports (CTR by industry), Unbounce Conversion Report (landing page benchmarks), WordStream (ad copy CTR/conversion data).

### Value Exchange
Revenue per word. Godzilla copy doesn't use more words — it uses fewer words that move more people. The difference between 1% and 8.8% conversion on the same traffic is the difference between surviving and compounding. Every word that doesn't cause movement is a word that costs money.

## D2: VISUAL & CREATIVE PRODUCTION
**Video, Design, Audio**

### Scoring Criteria

| # | Criterion | Pass (Komodo+) | Flag (Gecko) | Fail (Ant) |
|---|-----------|----------------|--------------|------------|
| 1 | **Resolution** | 4K master / 1080p minimum delivery | 720p delivery | <720p or upscaled artifacts |
| 2 | **Frame Rate** | 24fps cinematic / 30fps talking head / 60fps motion — correct for context | Correct fps but inconsistent | Wrong fps for format or variable frame rate |
| 3 | **Color Accuracy** | Rec.709 compliant, ΔE <3.0 | ΔE 3.0-5.0 | ΔE >5.0 — visible color shift |
| 4 | **Audio Peaks** | Voice: -6 to -12dB, no clipping | Occasional peaks above -6dB | Clipping, distortion, or voice below -18dB |
| 5 | **Noise Floor** | <-40dB | -35 to -40dB | >-35dB — audible hiss/hum |
| 6 | **Hook Timing** | Visual/verbal hook in <3 seconds | Hook at 3-5 seconds | No hook or hook after 5 seconds |
| 7 | **Thumbnail CTR** | 4-8% avg; 10%+ = Godzilla | 2-4% | <2% |
| 8 | **Accessibility** | Captions present, WCAG 2.1 AA color contrast | Captions present but unstyled | No captions, fails contrast requirements |

### Creature Example
- **Komodo:** 1080p talking head, 30fps, clean audio at -8dB, noise floor -42dB, hook at 2.5 seconds ("Most attorneys don't know this exists"), thumbnail CTR 6%. Technically clean, decent hook.
- **Godzilla:** 4K master with cinematic 24fps b-roll intercut, audio peaks at -7dB with -48dB noise floor, hook in 1.2 seconds (visual pattern interrupt + text overlay before speaker appears), thumbnail CTR 11%, auto-generated captions with brand-styled formatting. Every frame earns the next second of attention.

### Baselines
YouTube Creator Academy (thumbnail CTR benchmarks), Meta/LinkedIn video specs (resolution/format requirements), AES broadcast standards (audio levels), ITU-R BT.709 (color space compliance).

### Value Exchange
Trust compressed into seconds instead of minutes. A person decides in 3 seconds whether you're worth 30 more. Godzilla production doesn't just look better — it transfers credibility at the speed of perception. The same message in Ant production loses before the words even register.

## D4: STRATEGIC PLANNING
**Campaigns, Media Buying, Ecosystem Design**

### Scoring Criteria

| # | Criterion | Pass (Komodo+) | Flag (Gecko) | Fail (Ant) |
|---|-----------|----------------|--------------|------------|
| 1 | **Lever Sequence Logic** | Each lever feeds next with quantified handoff metric | Sequence exists but handoffs are vague | No sequence — disconnected tactics |
| 2 | **CPA by Channel** | Within range (FB $27-50, Google $50-100, LinkedIn $75-150 legal) with justification | CPA projected but no methodology shown | No CPA projection or wildly outside benchmarks |
| 3 | **ROAS Projection** | Documented methodology with assumptions stated | ROAS number exists but methodology unclear | No ROAS or "we'll see what happens" |
| 4 | **Budget Defensibility** | 100% allocated with rationale for every dollar | >80% allocated with rationale | Lump sums without breakdown |
| 5 | **Timeline Specificity** | Dates + owners + dependencies mapped | Dates exist, owners missing | "Q2" with no further detail |
| 6 | **Kill Criteria** | Per-channel thresholds defined (spend X without Y = stop) | Kill criteria exist for some channels | No kill criteria — money burns until someone notices |
| 7 | **The .00128 Move** | Identified — the micro-lever that creates disproportionate output | Partially identified | Not identified — plan is all 80% activity |
| 8 | **Measurement Architecture** | Leading + lagging indicators + monitoring cadence defined | Some metrics tracked, no cadence | No measurement plan |

### Creature Example
- **Komodo:** 90-day campaign plan. FB at $38 CPA, Google at $72 CPA, budget 100% allocated across 3 channels, monthly review cadence, kill criteria at 2x target CPA sustained 14 days. Solid plan that executes.
- **Godzilla:** Same channels but lever sequence mapped: content (Lever 1) → retargeting (Lever 2) → direct outreach (Lever 3), each handoff quantified (1,000 impressions → 40 clicks → 3 conversations). .00128 move identified: a single retargeting audience segment that converts at 3x the blended rate — 4% of budget driving 31% of pipeline. Kill criteria per channel per week. Measurement architecture with daily leading indicators, weekly lagging, monthly strategic review. Every cycle costs less than the last because the compounding is architected, not hoped for.

### Key Insight from ultimatestratabrain (Pinecone — 39K vectors)
**Channel Saturation Curves:** "These curves are crucial for recognising the saturation point of marketing channels. If a campaign reaches saturation, further investment may not deliver the same return." The platform tests budget adjustments incrementally (e.g., in $100 increments) to determine where each additional dollar is best allocated. **Every Godzilla-level Strategist must identify saturation points per channel — the moment where the next dollar produces less than the previous one.** This is the 70-150% constraint range: "maintain realism — you cannot suddenly shift 100% to the highest performer."

**The 74/29 Analytics Gap:** "74% of clients want to be data-driven but only 29% claim they are effective at turning analytics into actionable data." This 45-point gap is not a knowledge deficit — it's a framework deficit. The data exists. The desire exists. The bridge is missing. **A Godzilla Strategist doesn't just collect data — it converts data to action through structured decision frameworks.**

### Baselines
WordStream Legal Vertical (CPA/ROAS benchmarks), Clio Legal Trends Report (legal industry marketing spend patterns), Meta/Google/LinkedIn advertising platform data (auction dynamics, quality scores), ultimatestratabrain (saturation curves, 74/29 gap, forecasting benchmarks).

### Value Exchange
Decisions that compound — each cycle costs less than the last. Ant strategy spends the same dollar the same way every month and wonders why results plateau. Godzilla strategy identifies the .00128 move, funds it disproportionately, measures the compounding weekly, and kills everything that doesn't feed the sequence. The gap between "we ran ads" and "we engineered a revenue machine" is entirely in the planning.

## D5: HUMAN INFLUENCE
**Agreement Formation, Coaching, Community, Stage**

### Scoring Criteria

| # | Criterion | Pass (Komodo+) | Flag (Gecko) | Fail (Ant) |
|---|-----------|----------------|--------------|------------|
| 1 | **4-Step Completion** | Person reached Step 4 (commitment/action) | Reached Step 2-3 (understanding/desire) | Stuck at Step 1 or no framework applied |
| 2 | **ERI Progression** | Measurable shift from starting to ending state (documented) | Shift occurred but not measured | No observable emotional/relational change |
| 3 | **Objection Navigation** | Type identified (real vs smoke) + Formula-based response | Objection addressed but type not identified | Objection ignored, steamrolled, or conceded |
| 4 | **Listening Level** | Level 5 evidence — heard what wasn't said, named the invisible | Level 3 — active listening, accurate reflection | Level 1 — waiting to talk, missed emotional content |
| 5 | **Energy Match** | Correct archetype for scenario (Zeus, Athena, Hermes — matched to person) | Partially matched — right energy, wrong intensity | Mismatch — used authority when warmth was needed, or vice versa |
| 6 | **Silence Usage** | Held space after the ask — let the person arrive at their own yes | Paused but filled too soon | No silence — talked through the moment of decision |
| 7 | **Identity Elevation** | Person's self-concept visibly expanded — they see themselves differently | Mild encouragement but no identity shift | Person left same size they arrived |
| 8 | **Agreement Quality** | Commitment (person owns the action, internally driven) | Compliance (person agreed but isn't internally enrolled) | No agreement or false yes that won't hold |

### Creature Example
- **Komodo:** Coaching session reaches Step 3, identifies the person's core objection ("I don't have time" → actually fear of failure), listens at Level 4, energy matches the person's frustration with steady warmth, gets verbal agreement to take one zone action. Solid session — moved the needle.
- **Godzilla:** Session reaches Step 4. Within 8 minutes, names the invisible fear the person hasn't articulated ("You're not afraid of failing — you're afraid of succeeding and having nothing left to blame"). Holds 12 seconds of silence after. Person's energy shifts audibly. ERI documented: started at frustration/avoidance, ended at ownership/excitement. Agreement is a commitment — person texts the next day with results unprompted. Identity elevated: "I didn't know I was allowed to want that."

### Baselines
Sean's 20-output calibration set (gold standard for Formula-based influence), Adam's BAS session benchmarks (real coaching session scoring), ublib2 coaching vectors (4,000+ hours of decoded patterns).

### Value Exchange
Yes caused with integrity — agreements that hold, not compliance that unravels. The difference between Komodo and Godzilla influence isn't volume or pressure. It's precision. Godzilla influence names what the person can't see about themselves, holds space for the truth to land, and causes a yes that the person defends to others because it came from inside them.

## How to Use These Specs

**For Judging:** Score each criterion independently. The NET creature level is determined by the weakest criterion — one Ant score drags the whole domain to Ant, no matter how many Godzilla scores surround it. Weakest organ drags the net.

**For Growth:** Identify the single lowest-scoring criterion in each domain. That's the zone action. Not the average — the floor. Raise the floor and the whole domain rises.

**For Beings:** Every ACT-I being operating in a domain gets scored against these specs. No exceptions. The creature scale isn't aspirational — it's diagnostic. It tells you exactly where you are, not where you wish you were.
