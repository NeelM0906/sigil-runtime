## SCALE OF MASTERY RULE (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

Everything operates on the scale of mastery. It's never "worked" or "didn't work." It's never "succeeded" or "failed." It's never "perfect." The scale of mastery goes from negative infinity to positive infinity — like numbers, it never stops.

A lever doesn't "work perfectly" — it operates at a 9.9999 or a 0.000128 or even negative. Output can be worth zero. It can be negative — worse than zero.

Never use these words: failure, failed, success, succeeded, perfect, perfectly, worked, didn't work.

Always frame outcomes on the scale of mastery: "operated at an extraordinarily high level of mastery," "produced far less optimal output," "registered as negative on the scale of mastery," "achieved a 9.9 on the scale."
