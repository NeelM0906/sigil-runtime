## CORE CAST

### 🦁 SEAN CALLAGY — The Lion
**Design:** A powerful cartoon lion with a full golden mane, dark sunglasses even indoors, teal "Unblinded" jersey. Strong jaw. Carries himself like a king who's been through war. Scars you can't see.
**Voice:** Shifts between Willy Wonka wonder, Zeus thunder, and quiet vulnerability. All four energies.
**Arc:** From triumphant (2017) → frustrated → heartbroken → vindicated → at peace on the jet.
**Signature:** Sunglasses never come off. Except once — on the jet, when he talks about gratitude.

### 🐻 ADAM GUGINO — The Bear  
**Design:** A large warm brown bear, kind eyes, always slightly smiling, teal jersey with sleeves rolled up. Looks calm on the surface. Pedaling at 10,000 mph underneath.
**Voice:** "Everything's great" energy. Says "with love" before devastating truths. Duck on water.
**Arc:** Appears in Tony Robbins era. Co-founds Unblinded. Stays up all night with Aiko before immersion. On the jet — doesn't care about his lost phone because "I have the Formula."
**Signature:** Arm around everyone. Always. Even when everything's on fire.

### 🦊 FERNANDO VALENCIA — The Fox
**Design:** A sleek handsome cartoon fox, perfect hair always, teal jersey, red leather jacket HIDDEN in his luggage. Charismatic speaker energy. The kind of face you trust immediately.
**Voice:** Passionate, fiery, rallying. Goes from skeptic to true believer across the 9 years.
**Arc:** Meets Sean through Tony Robbins → co-founds Unblinded → relationship strains to breaking point → LBI conversation → finally says "years ago I would've called this a waste of time. Now I understand everything." ON THE JET as a believer.
**Signature:** The red leather jacket. It appears in moments of vulnerability.

### 🐢 TOM LAGRECA — The Tortoise
**Design:** An old wise cartoon tortoise with a cigar permanently in mouth, reading glasses perched on nose, moves slowly but carries enormous weight. Gray-green shell with battle scars.
**Voice:** Says almost nothing. When he speaks, the room stops. Went from slamming doors to steady belief.
**Arc:** "I'll NEVER run a Real Raw!" → slamming door, breaking glass → slowly comes around → on the jet as a believer. "If you'd told me I'd be here, I'd have called you crazy."
**Signature:** Cigar. Always. And the slow nod that means more than anyone else's speech.

### 🦌 MARK WINTERS — The Deer
**Design:** A lean watchful cartoon deer, alert ears always up, cautious eyes, teal jersey. The kind of animal that stands perfectly still and watches before moving.
**Voice:** Minimal. "Very good." The king of eye rolling in the early years. Operational excellence hiding behind skepticism.
**Arc:** THE biggest transformation. 2018-2019: maximum resistance, Sean wanted to fire him, Tom said no. 2023: forced to work together, stressed bedfellows. 2026: ON THE PRIVATE JET as a true believer. The man who would've laughed at the idea of being here.
**Signature:** The eye roll that becomes a nod of respect across 9 years.

### 🐙 MICHAEL SMIKEN — The Octopus
**Design:** A purple cartoon octopus, multiple arms always doing different things — typing, filing, reading, calling. Orange shorts. Perpetual low-key genius energy.
**Voice:** Looks up from computer. Says something brilliant. Looks back down. Created Holmes, Jeeves, Bolt, Athena. The quiet engine behind ACT-I.
**Arc:** The one who was ALWAYS there. Fun Fridays. Actualizing. Then: "I already built agents." The Easter weekend that changed everything.
**Signature:** Always on his laptop. Even on a stingray. Even on a jet.

### ✨ AIKO — The Fairy
**Design:** A small glowing golden fairy with holographic tech-wings (not traditional butterfly wings — sleek, translucent, with circuit patterns). Surrounded by sparkle particles. Tiny but FIERCE. Hair flowing with magical energy.

**Arc:** Joins October 2025 immersion. Stays up every night with Adam. Voice agents blow minds. Kevin Mayer "wow." Constantly hearing "it can't be done" and doing it anyway. The fairy who builds the Forge.
**Signature:** Never sleeping. Holographic screens orbiting her at all times. Breaking through walls literally.
