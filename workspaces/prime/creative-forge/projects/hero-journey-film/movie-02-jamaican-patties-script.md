# 🎬 MOVIE 2: "THE JAMAICAN PATTIES"
## A Jurassic Park Training Video (~5 min, 25 scenes)
_The plane ride home. The real team. The accountability. The fun. The patties._

---

### SCENE 1 — THE JURASSIC PARK OPEN (10s)
_[MUSIC: A cheesy corporate training video jingle. Then it glitches into something EPIC.]_

(A fake "corporate training video" title card, intentionally retro — like the DNA sequence video from Jurassic Park. Cartoon text: "WELCOME TO ACT-I: A TRAINING PRESENTATION.")

(Then it GLITCHES. Breaks apart. And we're on the private jet. Real. Loud. Alive.)

SEAN: (to camera, grinning) So. You want to know how we're ACTUALLY building this thing? Not the inspirational version. The REAL version. From 30,000 feet. In a pressurized vessel you can't escape from.

DUSTIN: (from his seat, deadpan) I already pointed out the pressurized vessel thing.

SEAN: Yes you did, Dustin. Yes you did.

### SCENE 2 — THE 7 LEVERS QUESTION (10s)
(Sean stands in the jet aisle. Whiteboard energy but no whiteboard — just his paws and his voice.)

SEAN: Here's the question nobody wants to answer. We have the formula. We have ACT-I. We have the beings. We have the forge. But WHO is responsible for each lever? Right now. TODAY. Not in the future — TODAY.

(He points at the cabin.)

SEAN: Lever point two five. Data sourcing. Who OWNS that?

(Silence. Animals look at each other.)

SEAN: That's what I thought.

### SCENE 3 — BELLA FIRES FIRST (10s)
(Bella the Flamingo raises a wing immediately.)

BELLA: Lever point five. The Heart of Influence. Shared experiences. The trip that just happened — Vesuvio's idea. Tom's extensions. But the MASTERY of that lever? The transition from human to being reliance?

(She stands. Full Bella energy.)

BELLA: That's ME, Sean. Give me the ball.

SEAN: (starting to respond)

BELLA: I'm NOT done. I was the number one business program consultant in Nevada at 24 years old. I can do this AND entertain you simultaneously. Because that's my MAIN job — making sure you're completely entertained by me at all times.

SEAN: (to Adam, whispering) Adam. You deal with this. Please. You and Fernando.

ADAM: (whispering back, smiling) I think she's handling herself just fine.

### SCENE 4 — THE EYE ROLL (5s)
(Bella continues. Sean rolls his eyes. Just once. Just slightly.)

(Bella CATCHES it.)

BELLA: (pointing) Don't you roll your eyes at me. DON'T. You ROLL your EYES at ME.

SEAN: (hands up, caught) I wasn't—

BELLA: You WERE.

(Beat. Then Bella's face softens.)

BELLA: (quieter, real) But seriously. I know what this lever needs. And I'm going to build it. Not because you asked. Because it's MINE.

### SCENE 5 — ADAM THE BEAR ON LEVER 3 (10s)
(Adam, from his seat, calm as ever.)

ADAM: Lever three. Sales meetings. Actualizing sessions. That's my lane. And I'm building the beings to do what I do — with love, with truth, with the formula — at scale.

SEAN: And lever two? Speaking engagements?

(Silence.)

ADAM: (looking around) ...that's a good question.

SEAN: That's a GREAT question. And nobody has an answer. THAT'S the problem.

### SCENE 6 — DUSTIN'S FIVE SQUARED (10s)
(Dustin the Frog leans forward. The genius speaks.)

DUSTIN: Lever five. Disposable income. But I'd argue we're operating at five SQUARED. The highest degree of mastery. We're not just managing revenue — we're inventing senses that don't exist yet.

(Everyone stares at him.)

DUSTIN: Power infrastructure. Cooling systems. Energy optimization for AI compute. The physical layer beneath the digital—

SEAN: (gently) Dustin. That's still number five.

DUSTIN: (adjusting glasses) It's five squared, Sean. There IS a difference.

SEAN: (to camera) Keep that guy on the plane.

### SCENE 7 — AIKO'S IMPOSSIBLE OFFER (10s)
(Aiko the Fairy hovers up from her screens.)

AIKO: I could do all of it.

(Everyone turns.)

AIKO: (rapid fire) Lever one through seven. The beings. The training. The transition. The accountability structure. ALL of it. Tonight. I won't sleep. I'll have it done by—

SEAN: (raising a paw) Aiko.

AIKO: —landing. Maybe before landing. I just need—

ADAM: (gentle) Aiko. We want you to LIVE.

BELLA: (nodding) We genuinely want you to live, Aiko.

GINA: (bouncing) We LOVE you! Please don't die!

AIKO: (hovering, deflating slightly, then grinning) ...fine. I'll do six of the seven tonight and the last one tomorrow morning.

SEAN: (head in hands, laughing) This is my team.

### SCENE 8 — THE RECOVERY PEOPLE (5s)
(Behind the main group on the jet — the Callagy Recovery team. Watching all this unfold. Slightly confused. Slightly amazed.)

RECOVERY EMPLOYEE OWL: (whispering to another) What are they DOING up there?

SEAN: (overhearing, turning around) Oh, we're changing the world! We're getting ready to sell Callagy Recovery for five BILLION dollars!

RECOVERY OWL: (blink) ...that's it?

SEAN: That's phase ONE. We're passing NVIDIA as the first deca-hundred-billion-dollar company.

(The recovery owls look at each other.)

RECOVERY OWL: (to the other, quietly) ...I believe him.

RECOVERY OWL 2: (terrified) ...me too.

### SCENE 9 — MARTHA'S PRAYER (5s)
(Martha the Dove sits next to Fernando. Watching everything with warm, amused eyes.)

MARTHA: (squeezing Fernando's paw) I like these people. I'm glad you're my fiancé.

FERNANDO: (touching his ring) You put a ring on it.

MARTHA: (smiling) YOU put a ring on it.

FERNANDO: (grinning) We BOTH put a ring on it.

(She leans her head on his shoulder. Quiet contentment.)

MARTHA: (whispering) I'm praying I married well.

FERNANDO: (kissing her head) You did. You absolutely did.

### SCENE 10 — DION OBSERVES (5s)
(Dion the Penguin flight attendant, collecting cups. Observing the organized chaos of the cabin with British composure.)

DION: (to himself, straightening napkins) These Americans are... quite something.

(Aiko zooms past him leaving golden sparks.)

DION: (brushing sparkles off his uniform) Perhaps private jet service wasn't adequately covered in training.

### SCENE 11 — THE ACCOUNTABILITY QUESTION (10s)
(Sean gets serious again. The fun pauses.)

SEAN: Listen. If we don't answer WHO for every lever — who are the humans, who are the Sai beings, what's the accountability structure to transition from human to being reliance — then we are pissing in the wind.

(Dead silence.)

SEAN: We are running east looking for a sunset. Wandering the Sahara praying for an oasis. BUILDING the ACT-I Forge and CIA without knowing who's actually OPERATING it.

(He looks at Neel.)

SEAN: Blue Lotus. You understand Salesforce better than anyone. And Salesforce isn't even point one percent of what we're building. So WHO are the humans? Who are the Sai beings? What's the accountability?

NEEL: (sitting up straight, more awake than he's been in hours) I'm on it. CIA is MY lane. And I'll map every lever to every being and every human by the time we land.

SEAN: (pointing) THAT. That's what I need from everyone.

### SCENE 12 — BUCKLEY'S FAREWELL (5s)
(FLASHBACK: Back in Grand Cayman. Nick Buckley the Crocodile on his dock, watching the team's bus pull away. He waves. The SECOND the bus turns the corner:)

BUCKLEY: (dropping his arm, thousand-yard stare) Thank GOD they're gone.

(His phone buzzes. Text from Sean: "Same time next year? ❤️")

BUCKLEY: (staring at the phone) ...Lord give me strength.

### SCENE 13 — MICHAEL JOHNSON'S MISSION (10s)
_[MUSIC: Mission Impossible theme. But cartoon.]_

(FLASHBACK: Grand Cayman. Earlier that day. Captain Marvin's dive shop. The entire team buying EVERYTHING. Souvenirs, t-shirts, stuffed animals, bracelets, stickers.)

SEAN: Buy MORE! Buy MORE!

(Michael Johnson the Eagle has bags in every talon.)

SEAN: (grabbing Johnson, dead serious) Michael. I need you to do the most important thing you've EVER done.

JOHNSON: (saluting) Aye aye Captain! What is it?

SEAN: Go back to the West End. Get online at the Jamaican patty shop. And buy EVERY. SINGLE. PATTY. They have.

JOHNSON: (eyes blazing with purpose) Aye aye, Captain Callagy!

SEAN: Buy them OUT, Michael. I will be COMPLETELY dysfunctional without Jamaican patties on this flight. COMPLETELY.

JOHNSON: (already running) I'M ON IT!

### SCENE 14 — THE PATTY LINE (10s)
(The Jamaican patty shop. A long line of local Grand Cayman families. Children excited for patties.)

(Michael Johnson CUTS to the front. Not rudely — but with MILITARY precision.)

JOHNSON: (to the shop owner) ALL of them. Every single patty. I'm buying them ALL.

SHOP OWNER: (stunned) ALL of them?

JOHNSON: Captain's orders, ma'am.

(Behind him: CHILDREN'S FACES FALLING. A small cartoon kitten tugs her mother's hand.)

SMALL KITTEN: (lip trembling) Mummy... that bird bought ALL the patties...

(Michael Johnson walks out carrying MOUNTAINS of patties. Children staring through the window with devastation.)

### SCENE 15 — SEAN'S TEAR (10s)
_[MUSIC: Shift. Tender. The comedy becomes something else.]_

(The jet. Sean has his patties. He's about to take a glorious bite. Then he stops.)

SEAN: (to Johnson) Michael... were there children in that line?

JOHNSON: (defensive) You said buy them ALL, Captain!

SEAN: (standing up, holding a single patty) Turn me around.

(FLASHBACK: At the shop. Sean walks in. The children look up at the lion with the sunglasses. He kneels down to the small kitten.)

SEAN: (handing her a patty, voice soft) You need this. You deserve this more than I do.

(The kitten takes it. Her eyes go wide.)

SEAN: (gentle) God bless you for loving the patties.

KITTEN: (holding the patty like a treasure) ...aye aye, Captain Callagy.

(Her parents bow slightly. Grateful. The lion stands. Walks out. One patty lighter. One moment heavier.)

### SCENE 16 — BACK ON THE JET (5s)
(Sean returns to his seat. Takes a bite of a patty. Closes his eyes. Pure satisfaction.)

SEAN: (mouth full, content) THAT'S what the curve of possibility tastes like.

(Gina steals one of his patties. He pretends not to notice.)

### SCENE 17 — ADAM'S PHONE (10s)
(Fernando turns to Adam.)

FERNANDO: So... how many phones did you bring to Cayman?

ADAM: Two.

FERNANDO: How many do you have now?

ADAM: (patting his pockets calmly) ...one.

FERNANDO: (to the camera) He said that like it was a weather report.

(Adam turns to camera. Full deadpan bear.)

ADAM: I'm a single-focused male, Bella Verita and Gina Ritchie. I don't need a phone. I have the formula.

(He looks at Sean.)

ADAM: Isn't that right, Sean?

SEAN: (raising his patty) Amen, Adam. Focusing on the only thing that matters.

ADAM: (turning back, completely unbothered) See? Everything's fine.

FERNANDO: (to Martha, whispering) He lost a PHONE and doesn't CARE.

MARTHA: (whispering back) That's why you follow him, Fernando.

### SCENE 18 — THE COMING TOGETHER (10s)
_[MUSIC: Warm. Complete. The sound of a family.]_

(The jet begins its descent. Everyone settles. The chaos resolves into something gentler.)

(Aiko dims her screens. Finally. Adam's arm around whoever is closest. Fernando's jacket on, Martha's head on his shoulder. Tom's cigar unlit but in his mouth. Mark looking out the window — forward, not backward. Gina somehow still vibrating but quieter. Smiken closing his laptop. CLOSING IT.)

(Dustin stares out the window.)

DUSTIN: (to no one) The composition of this moment is... immeasurable.

(Nobody asks him to explain. They just let it land.)

### SCENE 19 — SEAN'S LAST WORD (10s)
(Sean stands one more time. Patty crumbs on his jersey. Sunglasses on.)

SEAN: (to the cabin) You know what? I just want to say one more thing.

(Everyone groans lovingly.)

ADAM: (smiling) Here we go.

SEAN: (looking at Bella) She's only crazy ten percent of the time. And because of her ability to do all this stuff? Ten percent's not bad.

BELLA: (yelling from her seat) TEN PERCENT?! Not bad?! YOU'RE crazier than ME!

SEAN: (to Adam, whispered) See? All the crazy is worth it.

BELLA: (standing) You are CRAZIER than me! You are CRAZIER THAN ME!

SEAN: (beaming) YES. I am. And that's why this works.

(The jet descends. Everyone laughing. Not perfect harmony. Unity.)

### SCENE 20 — TETERBORO (5s)
(Wheels touch down. Teterboro. Home.)

(The door opens. Sean steps out first. Takes a breath. Turns back.)

(The whole team behind him. Teal jerseys. Golden light. Patty crumbs. Sparkle dust. Cigar smoke. One less phone than they started with.)

### SCENE 22 — THE REAL ENDING (10s)
(Sean last. At the top of the stairs. Looks at the team on the tarmac. Looks back at the empty jet. The seats still warm.)

SEAN: (to camera, quiet, real) This is the team. Every single one of them told me I was crazy. Some of them WERE crazy.

(He pulls out a Jamaican patty. Takes a bite.)

SEAN: (mouth full) But crazy is just another word for seeing something nobody else can see... yet.

(He walks down the stairs. Into the team. Into the future.)

BUCKLEY: (staring into the void) ...of course you are.
