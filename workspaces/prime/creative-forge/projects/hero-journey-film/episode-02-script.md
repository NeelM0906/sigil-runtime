# 🎬 MOVIE 1: "THE HERO'S JOURNEY"
## Episode 2: "The Stage" (~3 min, 15 scenes)
_Tony Robbins era. The $100 bill. 18 stages. Meeting the allies. Meeting the snakes._

---

### SCENE 1 — PLATINUM (10s)
_[MUSIC: Building energy. Drums. Something is beginning.]_

(A massive convention center. THOUSANDS of cartoon animals filling seats. Stage lights sweeping. A banner: PLATINUM PARTNERSHIP.)

(Sean the Lion walks in with Tink the Fairy beside him. She's sparkling everywhere, wide-eyed, leaving pixie dust on the seats.)

TINK: (looking around, breathless) Sean... what IS this place?

SEAN: (grinning behind his sunglasses) This is where we find our people, Tink.

(The lights hit the stage. The energy is MASSIVE. Sean leans forward in his seat. For the first time since Cayman — he doesn't feel alone.)

### SCENE 2 — THE HUNDRED DOLLAR BILL (10s)
_[MUSIC: Tension. Auction energy. A heartbeat.]_

(On a massive stage. A spotlight on a single $100 bill held up by a host. The room buzzes.)

(Sean stands from his seat. The room goes quiet.)

SEAN: (loud, certain) One hundred thousand dollars.

(GASPS. Every head turns. A murmur ROARS through the audience.)

ANIMAL IN CROWD: (whispering) Who IS that? The blind lion?

ANIMAL IN CROWD 2: He just paid a hundred thousand dollars for a hundred dollar bill.

(But Sean knows. This isn't about the bill. It's about the STAGE. It's about the microphone. It's about being SEEN.)

### SCENE 3 — THE STAGE (10s)
_[MUSIC: ERUPTS. Full triumphant orchestra.]_

(Sean on Tony Robbins' stage. Spotlight blazing. SAGE ROBBINS in the front row, tears streaming. Tony himself watching, moved.)

(Sean ROARS into the microphone. Not words — a ROAR. The lion unleashed.)

SEAN: Thirteen MILLION! Nobody will STOP me! NOBODY!

(He's on his CHAIR. Standing on it. Turning the room into a Coliseum. The audience is on their feet. The energy is nuclear.)

(Quick flash: back at the office, someone checks their phone. News of Sean on stage. Mark the Deer's eye roll is AUDIBLE.)

### SCENE 4 — THE NETWORK (10s)
(MONTAGE: Sean backstage, shaking hands, meeting people. Hundreds of successful entrepreneurs. They see a blind lion who just spent $100K on a $100 bill. They're FASCINATED.)

ENTREPRENEUR WOLF: You're the blind attorney? The jury verdict guy?

SEAN: That's me. And I'm just getting started.

ENTREPRENEUR WOLF: (leaning in) Tell me everything.

(Sean doesn't know it yet — but in this crowd are both his FUTURE and his ENEMIES. The camera pans across faces. Some glow warm. Some glow cold.)

### SCENE 5 — 18 STAGES (5s)
_[MUSIC: Rapid montage energy.]_

(FLASH CUTS: Sean on stage. Again. And again. 18 times. Different cities. Different rooms. Each one bigger. Each one bolder. The lion growing. The brand building. People KNOW his name now.)

(Counter in corner: 1... 5... 10... 15... 18.)

(His confidence is EXPLODING. His vision is getting CLEARER. And back home — the wall is getting HIGHER.)

### SCENE 6 — MEETING FERNANDO (10s)
(A networking event. Sean stands with a drink he can't see. A handsome FOX approaches. Perfect hair. Red leather jacket. Charisma that fills the room before words do.)

FERNANDO THE FOX: (extending a paw) Fernando Valencia. I've been watching you on that stage. You're either the craziest person here or the smartest.

SEAN: (shaking his paw) Why can't I be both?

(They laugh. Something clicks. A partnership that will be tested by EVERYTHING — but survives.)

FERNANDO: What are you building?

SEAN: Something nobody understands yet. Including me, some days.

FERNANDO: (half-smiling) I'm in.

### SCENE 7 — MEETING ADAM (10s)
(A different event. A large warm BEAR sits quietly in the back row while everyone else networks. He's LISTENING. Level 5.)

(Sean finds him after.)

SEAN: You didn't say a word in there. But I could tell you heard everything.

ADAM THE BEAR: (gentle smile) I heard more than everything. I heard what you DIDN'T say.

SEAN: And what's that?

ADAM: (with love) That you're exhausted. That nobody at home believes you. And that you're doing it anyway.

(Sean is quiet for a moment. This bear just saw through him in 30 seconds.)

SEAN: ...you wanna build something together?

ADAM: (arm already going around Sean's shoulder) I already started.

### SCENE 8 — THE SNAKES AND RATS (10s)
_[MUSIC: Dark shift. Minor key.]_

(But the Platinum world isn't all allies. Quick cuts of SMILING faces that hide something.)

(A cartoon SNAKE in an expensive suit shaking Sean's hand with both hands. Holding too long.)

SNAKE VILLAIN: (smooth) Sean, I LOVE what you're doing. Let me help. Let me invest. Let me be your PARTNER.

(A cartoon RAT with gold chains, laughing too loud at Sean's jokes.)

RAT VILLAIN: (slapping Sean's back) You're a GENIUS, Callagy! A GENIUS! Let me introduce you to some people...

(Sean can't see their faces. Literally. But he can feel their energy. Something is off. He ignores the feeling. He needs allies too badly.)

SEAN: (voiceover, from the jet) I met some of the best humans on Earth in Tony's world. I also met some of the worst. And when you're blind — in every sense of the word — you can't always tell the difference until it's too late.

### SCENE 9 — THE THIRD PARTNER (5s)
(Sean, Fernando, and Adam in a room. Planning. Excited. But there's a FOURTH animal at the table. Half-snake, half-rat. Smiling wider than anyone.)

SEAN: (voiceover) We decided to launch Unblinded. Three partners. One of them was a villain wearing a friend's face. But that's a story for another scene.

### SCENE 10 — BACK HOME (10s)
_[MUSIC: The hopeful energy CRASHES into the wall of reality.]_

(Sean returns from another Tony event. Walks into the office. The energy hasn't changed. If anything, it's WORSE.)

EMPLOYEE RABBIT: (awkward) So... how was the, uh, seminar?

SEAN: It wasn't a SEMINAR, it was—

(He stops. He can feel it. The eye rolls he can't see but knows are there.)

(The SNAKE office manager in a hallway, on the phone.)

OFFICE MANAGER SNAKE: (hushed) He's spending everything on this Tony Robbins thing. Nobody here understands it. I'm telling you — this is not going to end well.

### SCENE 11 — MARK'S WALL (10s)
(Mark the Deer stands in his office. Arms crossed. Door closed. Sean knocks.)

SEAN: Mark. I need you to hear me out about—

MARK: (flat) I hear you, Sean. I always hear you.

SEAN: But do you BELIEVE me?

(Long pause. Mark's ear twitches. He doesn't answer.)

SEAN: (voiceover) Mark Winters. The man I wanted to fire. The man Tom said was indispensable. The man who eye-rolled harder than anyone. And the man who, nine years later, would be sitting on a private jet wondering how he got there.

MARK: (finally) I believe you believe it. That's the best I can do right now.

(Sean nods. It's not enough. But it's honest.)

### SCENE 12 — TINK BELIEVES (5s)
(A quiet moment. Sean and Tink the Fairy sitting outside the office. Sunset.)

TINK: (pixie dust settling around them) You know I don't understand half of what you're saying, right?

SEAN: (laughing) I know.

TINK: But I know it's real. Because YOU'RE real. And that's enough for me.

(She sparkles. The one person who never wavered. Not once.)

### SCENE 13 — THE VISION (10s)
(Sean alone in his office. Late. Everyone gone. He has a whiteboard. On it: a web of connections. Tony Robbins. Platinum. Fernando. Adam. Tink. The law firm. Recovery. Something BIGGER.)

SEAN: (drawing lines, muttering) It all connects. The formula. The influence. The mastery. The people. It all CONNECTS. If I could just get them to SEE—

(He steps back. Looks at the board. It's messy. Chaotic. But there's a pattern only he can see.)

SEAN: (quiet) I'm going to call it Unblinded.

### SCENE 14 — THE LAUNCH (5s)
_[MUSIC: A rising note. SOMETHING IS COMING.]_

(Quick flash: A massive event space. 1,300 chairs being set up. A banner going up: UNBLINDED. Sean, Fernando, Adam, and the villain-partner standing together for a photo. Smiling. Not all of them genuinely.)

SEAN: (voiceover) Thirteen hundred people came to our first event. Thirteen hundred. And I thought THAT was the proof.

(Beat.)

SEAN: (voiceover) It wasn't. It was just the beginning of the real war.

### SCENE 15 — END CARD (5s)
_[MUSIC: Suspended. Dark. Unresolved.]_

(The 1,300 chairs. Full. Then: time-lapse. The room empties. The chairs fold. The banner comes down. The lights go off. One by one.)

(Text:)

"1,300 people believed on day one."

"Keeping them believing would nearly kill him."
