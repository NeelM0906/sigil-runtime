### GTM Strategy Patterns for Premium Coaching Programs
- Heart of Influence (Lever 1 Ecosystem Merging) consistently ranks as highest-ROI channel for premium ($25K+) programs due to trust velocity through O's and B's
- ACT-I beings can replace traditional SDR functions at the 0.8% efficiency tier — key differentiator vs. conventional outreach
- Diagnostic-first enrollment (replacing sales calls with ACT-I being diagnostic sessions) is a high-conviction strategy: show prospects their contamination, let truth do the closing
- 90-day launch windows for premium programs should target founding cohorts of 12-15 members to build proof and testimonials before scaling
- Lever 0.5 (Shared Experiences) is the critical bridge between content/speaking (Lever 2) and actual enrollment — without it, conversion drops significantly
- Psychographic targeting matters more than demographic for Unblinded-style programs: the ideal client has already invested heavily in growth and hit an unnamed ceiling
