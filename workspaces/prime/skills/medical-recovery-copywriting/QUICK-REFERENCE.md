# Medical Recovery Copywriting — Quick Reference Card

## The Unblinded Language Rules

| ❌ NEVER SAY | ✅ SAY INSTEAD |
|-------------|----------------|
| prospect | person / provider |
| sales | revenue |
| closing | reaching agreement |
| closed won | agreement reached |
| closer | Agreement Maker |
| pitch | share / present |
| target | serve |

---

## The Hook Library

### Credibility Hooks
- "28 years fighting for healthcare providers"
- "Nearly $1 billion recovered"
- "Backed by Callagy Law — real legal muscle"

### Results Hooks
- "85-95% arbitration success rate"
- "800-1400% more than original EOB"
- "85% collected by month eight"

### Loss Aversion Hooks
- "How much money are you letting insurance companies STEAL?"
- "Money on the table if claims go unchallenged"
- "Insurance companies count on you not fighting back"

### Risk Elimination Hooks
- "No upfront fees. If we don't win, you pay nothing."
- "We front all filing fees and arbitration costs"
- "We only get paid when you get paid"

## The 4-Step Model (Quick)

1. **Emotional Rapport** — Feel their pain first
2. **Truth + Pain + Yes Strategy** — Help them see what they can't see
3. **Heroic Identity** — Why you're different (legal warriors, not billers)
4. **Agreement Formation** — Same side of the table, not closing

## Quick Objection Responses

**"We have someone handling that."**
→ "Are they using NSA IDR to get 800-1400% above EOB? Most aren't."

**"Too good to be true."**
→ "That's what insurance companies want you to think. Send us claims — we'll prove it."

**"Send an email."**
→ "Happy to! What's been your biggest challenge getting paid fairly?"
