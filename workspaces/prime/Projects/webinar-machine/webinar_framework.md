## Webinar Structure (All Three)

### Pre-Webinar (Registration → Live)
- **Day -14:** Registration opens
- **Day -7:** Early bird bonuses end
- **Day -3:** "Seats filling up" urgency
- **Day -1:** Final reminder + agenda preview
- **Day 0:** 2-hour reminder, 1-hour reminder, 15-min reminder

### During Webinar (90 Minutes)
| Segment | Duration | Purpose |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Hook + Promise | 5 min | Attention capture, outcome preview |
| Credibility | 5 min | Why listen to us |
| Problem Agitation | 15 min | Pain points, cost of inaction |
| Content Delivery | 30 min | Actual value, methodology reveal |
| Case Studies | 15 min | Proof of concept, social proof |
| Offer Reveal | 10 min | Clear CTA, pricing, bonuses |
| Q&A + Close | 10 min | Objection handling, urgency |

### Post-Webinar (Replay → Conversion)
- **Hour +1:** Replay link + offer recap
- **Day +1:** Case study deep dive email
- **Day +2:** FAQ + objection handling
- **Day +3:** Scarcity/deadline reminder
- **Day +5:** Final call + bonus expiration
- **Day +7:** "We noticed you didn't..." last chance

## Conversion Optimization Principles

### 1. The Offer Stack
Every webinar ends with a structured offer stack:
- **Core Offer:** The main product/service
- **Bonus 1:** Time-limited fast-action bonus
- **Bonus 2:** Exclusive webinar-only bonus
- **Bonus 3:** Risk reversal / guarantee
- **Bonus 4:** Community/ongoing support access

### 2. Price Anchoring
- Show the "value" first ($50,000 in value)
- Reveal "investment" second (Today only: $5,000)
- Payment plans always available
- "No-brainer" positioning

### 3. Urgency Triggers
- **Scarcity:** Limited spots, cohort caps
- **Deadline:** Bonuses expire at midnight
- **Social Proof:** "47 people just registered"
- **Loss Aversion:** "Here's what you'll miss..."

### 4. Objection Preemption
Build into presentation:
- "I know what you're thinking..."
- "You might be wondering if this works for [specific situation]..."
- "The #1 question we get..."

## Technical Requirements

### Platform Stack
- **Webinar Software:** Demio or WebinarJam (supports automation + live)
- **Landing Pages:** Unbounce or custom (for split testing)
- **Email:** ActiveCampaign or ConvertKit (automation + tagging)
- **CRM:** HubSpot (deal tracking, lead scoring)
- **Analytics:** GA4 + UTM tracking + custom events

### Tracking Requirements
- Registration source (UTM parameters)
- Landing page variant
- Email sequence variant
- Show-up rate
- Watch time percentages
- Offer click-through
- Application/purchase completion
- Revenue per registrant

## Risk Mitigation

1. **Tech Failure:** Always have backup stream, phone dial-in option
2. **Low Show-up:** SMS reminders, aggressive email sequence
3. **Poor Conversion:** Live chat support during offer, immediate follow-up calls
4. **Ad Fatigue:** 3+ creative variants per audience segment
5. **Compliance:** Legal review all claims, testimonial disclosures
