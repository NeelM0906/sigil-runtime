### PHASE 2: ACCELERATION (Days 22-60)
**Target: 200-250 enrollments**
**Energy: Aspirational + Goddess (nurture)**
**Primary source: Scaled paid + JV partnerships + challenges**

#### The Heart of Influence Engine (Lever 1: Ecosystem Merging)

This is where the Unblinded approach diverges most dramatically from conventional marketing.

**Conventional:** Run more ads. Spend more money. Optimize CPAs.

**Unblinded:** Activate the O's and B's.

| Role | Definition | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **O's (Audience Owners)** | People who have audiences that overlap with ideal Visionary enrollees | Invite them to a Shared Experience (Lever 0.5). Let them EXPERIENCE the transformation. Then invite them to share with their audience — not as affiliates, but as advocates. |
| **B's (Connectors)** | People who bridge ecosystems, know the O's, open doors | Build genuine relationships. Reciprocity (Element 7). Give before asking. |

**Heart of Influence flips the frame:** We're not asking for promotion. We're offering a platform. "We want YOUR audience to experience what we've built. Here's how we make that easy for you."

**The 5-Day Challenge Model (Dean Graziosi framework, Unblinded-ized):**

Run 2-3 live challenges during Phase 2 — each designed as a micro-experience of the Visionary Program:

- **Day 1:** Identify the 7 Liberators/Destroyers in your life right now (Self Mastery diagnostic)
- **Day 2:** The one conversation that would change everything — and why you're avoiding it (Influence Mastery teaser)
- **Day 3:** Your Zone Action vs. your 80% activity — find the 0.8% move (Process Mastery)
- **Day 4:** Live hot-seat with Sean — watch the formula in real time
- **Day 5:** The path forward — Visionary Program enrollment for those who are ready

Each challenge day is designed using the 4 Steps:
1. **Emotional Rapport** — meet them where they are, not where we want them to be
2. **Truth and Pain** — help them see what they can't see about their own patterns
3. **Heroic Unique Identity** — show them who they're capable of becoming
4. **Agreement Formation** — invite commitment, not compliance

**Paid Media Scaling (Phase 2):**
- Budget: 50% of total campaign budget
- Scale winning ad sets from Phase 1 (>2x ROAS threshold)
- Launch YouTube ads alongside Meta — YouTube for longer-form, trust-building content
- Retarget all Phase 1 webinar/challenge attendees who didn't enroll
- **Creative rotation calendar:** New hooks every 7-10 days, new thumbnails every 10-14, full creative refresh at Day 45
- **Kill vs. Optimize (Recovery's framework):** If an ad set exceeds 2x target CPA for 72 hours with 50+ impressions, kill it. If it's within 1.5x CPA, optimize (audience, placement, creative — one variable at a time).

**Resilience Checkpoint (Day 45 — Mid-Campaign Review):**
- Are we at 150+ enrollments? If yes, stay course. If no, diagnose:
  - Funnel drop-off analysis — where are we losing people?
  - Ad fatigue check — are CTRs declining?
  - Show-up rate audit — are registrants attending?
  - Offer review — is pricing misaligned with perceived value?
- **Emergency intervention options:** Bonus offer for limited window, add a live Q&A with Sean, deploy testimonial blitz from Phase 1 enrollees

**Target: 150-175 enrollments**
**Energy: Zeus + Goddess**
**Primary source: Referrals + compounding testimonials + close urgency**

#### The Referral Engine

By Phase 3, you have 275-350 enrolled members. They are your most powerful marketing channel.

**Referral mechanics (Unblinded-ized):**
- Every enrolled member gets a "Share Your Transformation" kit — not an affiliate link, a *story framework* for sharing what's changed
- Incentive: not discounts (cheapens the program). Instead: exclusive access, additional coaching, recognition
- **The viral coefficient target:** Each enrolled member refers 0.3-0.5 people. At 300 enrolled members, that's 90-150 referral leads, of which 50-75 enroll
- **Element 7 (Reciprocity) in action:** Give first. Over-deliver in the program. The referrals are the natural overflow of genuine transformation.

#### The Close Sequence

**Real scarcity, not manufactured:**
- Cohort capacity is fixed. When it's full, it's full. Communicate this honestly.
- Sean's time is finite. More enrollees means less individual attention. This is true. Say it.
- The program starts on a specific date. After that date, entry requires waiting for the next cohort. This creates genuine urgency.

## PART IV: CHANNEL STRATEGY

### Tier 1 — Primary Scale Engines (70% of budget)

**Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram)**
- Best for: Awareness, webinar registrations, retargeting
- Creative: Short-form video (15-60s), carousel testimonials, story ads
- Targeting: Lookalike audiences from existing community, interest-based (entrepreneurship, personal development, coaching), and retargeting pools
- Budget pacing: Start at $200-500/day, scale winners to $2,000-5,000/day by Phase 2

**YouTube Ads**
- Best for: Trust-building, longer consideration cycle
- Creative: 2-5 minute testimonial/teaching ads (skippable in-stream)
- Targeting: Intent-based (searching for business coaching, personal transformation, similar programs)
- Budget pacing: Start at $100-300/day, scale to $1,000-2,000/day

### Tier 2 — Relationship Engines (20% of budget)

**Email Marketing**
- Sequence architecture: Welcome → Nurture → Challenge invitation → Application → Close
- Frequency: 3-5x/week during active launch phases, 1-2x/week during sustain
- Key: Segment aggressively. Someone who attended a webinar gets a different sequence than someone who only opted in.

**JV / Partnership (Heart of Influence)**
- Zero ad spend — relationship investment
- Target: 10-20 O's with audiences of 10,000-100,000+ in adjacent spaces
- Mechanism: Shared Experience → Co-hosted event → Audience sharing
- Expected yield: 100-150 enrollments from partnerships alone

**Community / Organic Social**
- Sean's personal content: daily, authentic, unscripted
- ACT-I being demonstrations: show Athena, Callie, Mira in action
- User-generated content from enrolled members

### Tier 3 — Amplification (10% of budget)

**Podcast Guesting** — Sean on 10-15 relevant podcasts during the 90 days
**LinkedIn** — For B2B-adjacent audience (executives, founders)
**TikTok** — Short-form clips from Sean's teachings, challenges, live events
