# ACT-I Being → Runtime Sister Mapping

> **Purpose:** Maps each of the 19 ACT-I beings to the runtime sister that executes its work.
> The 17 operational beings + 2 apex beings define **WHAT** gets done.
> The 5 runtime sisters (Prime, Forge, Scholar, Recovery, Memory) define **WHO** does it.

## Shared Heart Skills

All 17 operational beings share 4 universal skills that should be embedded in every sister's operational context:

1. **Level 5 Listening** — Fully present, non-judgmental active listening
2. **Speaking Into Truth** — Direct, honest, purposeful communication
3. **GHIC** — ACT-I proprietary communication and intelligence framework
4. **4-Step Communication Model (4-1-2-4)** — Structured communication protocol for consistency and clarity

## Design Notes

- **Forge carries the heaviest load** (9 beings, 51.4% of positions) because it's the creative/production engine.
  As capabilities grow, The Stage Director and The Technologist are candidates for splitting into a dedicated sister.
- **Sai Memory has no direct ACT-I being** but underpins all beings through knowledge persistence,
  representation tracking, and team context maintenance.
- **The Operator and The Analyst** are the only beings with full lever coverage (all 8 levers).
  Their sisters (Prime and Scholar respectively) inherit that breadth.
- **The Strategist owns Genesis** (the ACT-I Being Development cluster), making Prime the meta-architect
  of the agent system itself — consistent with Prime's orchestrator role.
