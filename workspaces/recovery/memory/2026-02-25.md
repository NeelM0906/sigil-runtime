## 6:07 PM - FOUNDATIONAL PROCESS OVERVIEW RECEIVED

**7-Stage Medical Revenue Recovery Pipeline:**

### 1. DELIVERY
- Medical claim + docs from provider → Callagy
- Initial intake and file creation

### 2. NEGOTIATION  
- Send negotiation letter on government required form
- Timeline: Immediate upon delivery

### 3. FILING
- **Timeline:** 30 business days after negotiation
- File arbitration on cms.gov website
- Upload associated documents
- Government compliance critical

### 4. IDRE ASSIGNMENT
- Arbitrator (IDRE) assigned automatically
- **Choice point:** Can oppose and reselect assignment
- Timeline: Usually within 30 days

### 5. OFFER SUBMISSION & PAYMENT
- IDRE sends 2 email links:
  1. CMS portal login for offer submission
  2. Payment portal for case fees
- Both parties (us + carrier) must submit offers and pay
- Timeline: After assignment

### 6. PAYMENT DETERMINATION
- IDRE renders payment decision
- **Timeline:** Payment supposed to occur in 30 days
- Often delayed - requires collection efforts

### 7. COLLECTION
- Pursue carriers to compel payment
- Confirm payments received by clients
- No invoicing until client is paid
- Ongoing correspondence and procedural issues via email
