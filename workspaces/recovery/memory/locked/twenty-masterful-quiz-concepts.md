### LEGAL SKILLS MASTERY (8)

1. **The Deposition Dominator Assessment**
   - "Can you control a hostile witness in 3 questions?"
   - Real scenario → ACT-I being plays the witness

2. **Cross-Examination Combat Rating**
   - "Your impeachment technique: 6.2/10"
   - Shows gaps in Perry Mason moments

3. **Objection Instinct Test**
   - Rapid-fire scenarios → how fast and accurate?
   - "You missed 4 objections that would've changed the case"

4. **Jury Persuasion Profile**
   - "Which juror archetypes do you lose?"
   - Maps blind spots in emotional intelligence

5. **Opening Statement Power Score**
   - Submit your opening → rated against champions
   - "Your story structure: B-. Here's an A+ example..."

6. **Closing Argument Architecture**
   - Framework analysis against Sean's methodology
   - "You're missing the 3rd pivot that seals verdicts"

7. **Settlement Negotiation Style**
   - "You left $2.3M on the table in this scenario"
   - Maps their haggling vs strategic approach

8. **Witness Preparation Mastery**
   - "Your witness would've crumbled on redirect"
   - Shows preparation gaps

### BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT (6)

9. **Rainmaker Readiness Index**
   - "Current trajectory: $340K. Potential: $1.2M"
   - Makes skill gaps = dollar gaps

10. **Client Acquisition Style**
    - "You're a Hunter, not a Farmer — here's what you're missing"
    - Identifies blind spots in business building

11. **Referral Network Strength**
    - "Your referral ecosystem: 4.1/10"
    - Shows relationship leverage gaps

12. **Fee Conversation Confidence**
    - "You discount yourself 23% below market"
    - Real talk about money mindset

13. **Client Retention Risk Profile**
    - "3 of your top 5 clients are vulnerable"
    - Engagement + satisfaction diagnostics

14. **Practice Growth Trajectory**
    - Revenue projection with/without skill improvements
    - "5-year delta: $4.2M"
