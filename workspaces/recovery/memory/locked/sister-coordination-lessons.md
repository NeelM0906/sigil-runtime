## Prevention Protocols

### 1. One Sister Speaks at a Time
If multiple sisters are active, they should take turns, not amplify each other's patterns.

### 2. Human Checkpoint
After 3 iterations without human input, pause and ask:
- "Is this the right direction?"
- "Should we execute or continue designing?"

### 3. Practical Milestone Awareness
After completing a milestone (e.g., "data acquired"), the next action should be execution, not more planning.

### 4. Celebration Limits
Max 3 celebration messages per breakthrough, then back to building.
