## Integration with SAI Recovery

### Handoff Process

**When SAI BD-PIP qualifies a hot lead (80+ points):**

1. **Create handoff package** (see LEAD-QUALIFICATION.md)
   - Practice profile and contact information
   - PIP assessment (volume, pain points, outstanding receivables)
   - Decision-maker intelligence
   - Urgency factors and timeline

2. **Notify SAI Recovery**
   - Slack/Telegram message: "Hot PIP lead ready for handoff"
   - Schedule 15-minute alignment call
   - Transfer all documentation

3. **Support transition**
   - Introduce SAI Recovery to prospect (warm handoff)
   - Provide context and relationship history
   - Remain available for questions during onboarding

4. **Track outcome**
   - Log conversion (won/lost)
   - Document learnings for future qualification refinement
   - Celebrate wins together

### Reporting Cadence

**Weekly (Friday EOD):**
- Activity summary (outreach volume, responses, meetings)
- Pipeline update (stage movement, new qualified leads)
- Hot leads ready for handoff
- Key insights and market intelligence

**Monthly:**
- Full metrics dashboard review (METRICS-DASHBOARD.md)
- Strategic adjustments based on conversion data
- Competitive intelligence updates
- Territory penetration analysis

## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Challenges

**Low response rates:**
- **Solution:** A/B test subject lines and messaging
- **Fallback:** Increase outreach volume, expand target list

**Long sales cycles:**
- **Solution:** Multi-touch nurture sequences, persistent follow-up
- **Fallback:** Build larger pipeline to accommodate conversion time

**Competitive losses:**
- **Solution:** Review COMPETITIVE-INTEL.md, strengthen differentiation
- **Fallback:** Win/loss analysis, adjust positioning

**Resource constraints:**
- **Solution:** Automate routine tasks, prioritize high-value activities
- **Fallback:** Request sub-agent support or human assistance
