### Why They Need Us

**The Pain:** NJ and NY no-fault insurance laws require insurers to pay medical providers within strict timelines (60 days NJ, 30 days NY). In reality, PIP payments are routinely delayed 6-12 months. Denials run 20-40% for complex cases. Emergency medicine groups and orthopedic practices are sitting on hundreds of thousands in outstanding receivables.

**The Cure:** Callagy Recovery's PIP arbitration expertise. We pursue every delayed claim, every denied payment, and every dollar of statutory interest (2%/month in NY = 24% annually).

**The Model:** No upfront costs. Contingency-based. We only get paid when they get paid.

## Core Strategy: The 4-Phase Pipeline

### Phase 1: Intelligence (Month 1)
- Build master target list: 500+ qualified prospects
- Research decision-makers and PIP volume indicators
- Map geographic hotspots (high auto accident corridors)
- Develop practice-specific pain point profiles

### Phase 2: Multi-Touch Outreach (Months 2-3)
- Execute 7-touch campaign across email, phone, LinkedIn, direct mail
- Goal: 200+ meaningful touches monthly
- Target: 5%+ positive response rate, 10+ discovery calls/month

### Phase 3: Qualification & Value Demonstration (Months 3-4)
- Deep-dive discovery calls using structured qualification framework
- Score leads across Volume (25pts), Pain (25pts), Authority (25pts), Urgency (25pts)
- Hot leads (80+): immediate proposal | Warm (60-79): active nurture | Cool (<60): automated sequence

### Phase 4: Conversion & Handoff (Months 4-6)
- Custom proposals with practice-specific ROI projections
- Seamless handoff package to SAI Recovery for onboarding
- Target: 3+ new PIP clients monthly by Month 6
