# STRATEGY.md - PIP Business Development Master Plan

## Executive Summary

**Objective:** Convert 100+ emergency medicine and orthopedic practices in NJ & NY into Callagy Recovery PIP arbitration clients by Q4 2026.

**Target Revenue:** $2.5M+ annual contract value from PIP specialization vertical.

**Core Insight:** PIP payment delays are crushing cash flow for auto accident care providers. Callagy Recovery's arbitration expertise is the precision weapon they need.

## Market Analysis

### New Jersey PIP Market

**Payment Timeline Crisis:**
- **Legal requirement:** 60-day payment (45-day extension allowed)
- **Reality:** 6-12 month delays common
- **Provider impact:** $50K-$200K in outstanding receivables per practice
- **Interest penalty:** Available but rarely collected without legal action

**Prime Targets:**
- **Emergency medicine groups:** 150+ practices statewide
- **Orthopedic practices:** 80+ trauma-focused practices
- **Geographic hotspots:** North Jersey (auto accident volume), Shore counties (summer spikes)

### New York PIP Market

**Payment Timeline Crisis:**
- **Legal requirement:** 30-day payment
- **Reality:** 2% monthly interest accumulates during delays
- **Arbitration backlog:** 10-month wait for AAA hearings
- **Provider impact:** $100K-$500K in outstanding receivables per practice

**Prime Targets:**
- **Emergency medicine groups:** 200+ practices statewide
- **Orthopedic practices:** 120+ trauma-focused practices
- **Geographic hotspots:** NYC metro, Long Island, Hudson Valley

## Ideal Client Profile

### Emergency Medicine Groups

**Demographics:**
- **Size:** 10+ physicians, 5,000+ annual auto accident cases
- **Setting:** Hospital-based EDs, urgent care centers
- **Revenue:** $2M-$10M annual PIP billing
- **Pain point:** 20-40% of PIP claims delayed/denied

**Decision-makers:**
- Practice administrator/revenue cycle manager
- Medical director with business authority
- Hospital CFO (for hospital-employed groups)

### Orthopedic Practices

**Demographics:**
- **Size:** 5+ physicians, 2,000+ annual auto accident cases
- **Specialties:** Spine, sports medicine, trauma
- **Revenue:** $1M-$5M annual PIP billing
- **Pain point:** Complex cases = higher denial rates

**Decision-makers:**
- Practice owner/managing partner
- Practice administrator
- Revenue cycle director
