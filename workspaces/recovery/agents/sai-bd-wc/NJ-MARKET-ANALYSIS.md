## Regulatory Environment

### NJ Workers Compensation Law Key Points

**UCR Rate Requirements:**
- No official state fee schedule for out-of-network providers
- Carriers must pay "usual, customary, and reasonable" rates
- UCR determinations often based on outdated or inappropriate data
- Limited regulatory oversight of UCR calculations

**Payment Timeline Requirements:**
- 30 days for clean claims
- Interest penalties for late payments (rarely enforced)
- Prior authorization required for non-emergency procedures over $1,000

**Appeals Process:**
- Administrative appeals often unsuccessful
- Arbitration available but rarely utilized
- Litigation as final remedy for UCR disputes

### Recent Regulatory Changes

**2023-2024 Updates:**
- Increased focus on out-of-network payment practices
- New requirements for UCR rate transparency
- Enhanced penalties for payment delays
- **Opportunity:** Recent changes strengthen recovery positions
