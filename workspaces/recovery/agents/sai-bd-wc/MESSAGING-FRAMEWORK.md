## Objection Handling Framework

### Objection 1: "We don't have time for this"

**Response Strategy:**
"I completely understand - running a surgical practice is incredibly demanding. That's exactly why we handle everything on a contingent fee basis. You invest zero time upfront. We do all the analysis, documentation, and recovery work. You only hear from us when there's money to collect."

**Supporting Evidence:**
- "Our process requires minimal practice involvement"
- "We use existing billing records - no new documentation needed"
- "Average time investment: 2 hours total over 6-month recovery period"

### Objection 2: "We're satisfied with our current payments"

**Response Strategy:**
"That's great that you feel good about your current situation. Many practices don't realize they're entitled to higher UCR rates. Our analysis is free - worst case, you confirm you're being paid fairly. Best case, we identify hundreds of thousands in recoverable underpayments."

**Data-Driven Follow-up:**
- "95% of NJ surgical practices we analyze have significant underpayments"
- "Carriers don't voluntarily disclose UCR rate methodologies"
- "Free analysis takes 15 minutes and could identify 6-figure recovery opportunities"

### Objection 3: "We already have legal representation"

**Response Strategy:**
"Excellent - having legal support shows you understand the importance of protecting your practice's interests. Are they specifically handling UCR rate recovery and 8-year lookbacks? That's our exclusive focus. Most firms handle injury cases but not provider-side UCR rate disputes."

**Specialization Advantage:**
- "UCR rate recovery requires specific expertise in provider-side law"
- "We work collaboratively with existing legal counsel"
- "Our contingent fee structure adds no cost to current legal relationships"

### Objection 4: "We tried this before and it didn't work"

**Response Strategy:**
"I appreciate you sharing that experience. What type of recovery was attempted? Most efforts focus on recent claims or administrative appeals. Our approach is different - we pursue UCR rate litigation with 8-year lookback authority. The legal landscape has also changed significantly in the past few years."

**Differentiation Points:**
- "Litigation-backed approach vs. administrative appeals"
- "8-year lookback vs. recent claims only"
- "UCR rate expertise vs. general collection services"
- "Recent regulatory changes strengthen provider positions"

### Objection 5: "The contingent fee is too high"

**Response Strategy:**
"I understand the fee structure is an important consideration. Let me put it in perspective - if we recover nothing, you pay nothing. If we recover $300K, you net $200K that you wouldn't have otherwise. The alternative is receiving zero from underpayments you're currently unaware of."

**Value Demonstration:**
- "Compare 67% of something vs. 100% of nothing"
- "Our fee only applies to money we actually collect"
- "Historical success rate justifies contingent fee structure"
- "No upfront costs protects your cash flow"
