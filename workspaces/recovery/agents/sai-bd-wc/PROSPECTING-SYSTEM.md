- Professional social media profiles (LinkedIn, practice pages)
- Court records (existing litigation patterns)

### Target Segmentation Matrix

**Tier 1: Priority Targets (Immediate Pursuit)**
- Out-of-network surgical providers
- High WC volume indicators (20+ cases/month)
- Limited current legal representation
- Geographic concentration in target counties
- Strong financial indicators (multiple locations, staff size)

**Tier 2: Secondary Targets (Follow-up Campaign)**
- Medium WC volume indicators (10-19 cases/month)
- Mixed network status (some out-of-network contracts)
- Established practices with growth patterns
- Suburban locations with industrial worker base

**Tier 3: Long-term Prospects (Nurture Campaign)**
- Newer practices building WC volume
- Recently dropped from insurance networks
- Practices considering out-of-network strategies
- Referral sources for Tier 1 targets

### Channel 2: Phone Prospecting

**Cold Call Strategy:**

**Gatekeeper Script (30 seconds):**
"Hi, I'm calling from Callagy Recovery about Workers Compensation underpayments. We help surgical practices recover money they're owed from insurance carriers. Is [Decision Maker] available for a brief conversation about UCR rate recovery?"

**Decision Maker Script (60 seconds):**
"Dr. [Name], I'm calling because our analysis shows surgical practices like yours are typically underpaid 30-40% on Workers Compensation cases. We specialize in recovering those underpayments on a contingent fee basis - you pay nothing unless we collect. We've recovered millions for NJ providers, including an 8-year lookback on historical underpayments. Would you be open to a 15-minute conversation about your WC payment patterns?"

**Objection Handling:**
- "We don't have time" → "I understand. Can we schedule 15 minutes next week when it's more convenient?"
- "We're happy with current payments" → "That's great. Many practices don't realize they're entitled to higher UCR rates. Our analysis is free - worst case, you confirm you're being paid fairly."
- "We have other representation" → "Excellent. Are they specifically handling UCR rate recovery and 8-year lookbacks? That's our specialty."

**Call Timing:**
- Tuesdays-Thursdays: 8:30-10:00 AM, 2:00-4:00 PM
- Avoid Mondays (administrative catch-up) and Fridays (early close)
- End of month (billing focus period)

## Lead Qualification Process

### Initial Screening Criteria

**Must-Have Qualifications:**
- Out-of-network status with major WC carriers
- Minimum 10 WC cases monthly (estimated)
- Located in target NJ counties
- Decision-maker access confirmed
- No current WC recovery representation

**Disqualifying Factors:**
- Full in-network contracting with all major carriers
- Less than 5 WC cases monthly
- Existing comprehensive WC recovery services
- Financial instability indicators
- Hostile to recovery services

### Discovery Call Framework (45 minutes)

**Section 1: Practice Overview (10 minutes)**
- Practice history and growth
- Physician specialties and procedures
- Patient demographics and referral sources
- Current WC volume and trends

**Section 2: WC Payment Analysis (15 minutes)**
- Current WC carriers and payment patterns
- Typical payment amounts vs. billed charges
- Payment timeline and collection issues
- Prior authorization and denial patterns

**Section 3: Financial Impact Assessment (10 minutes)**
- Annual WC revenue estimates
- Cash flow impacts from underpayments
- Current collection costs and efforts
- Opportunity cost of delayed payments

**Section 4: Historical Opportunity Review (10 minutes)**
- 8-year lookback potential
- Previous recovery efforts
- Documentation and records availability
- Timeline for potential recovery

**Qualification Scorecard (100 points total):**
- WC volume (30 points): 30+ monthly = 30 pts, 20-29 = 20 pts, 10-19 = 10 pts
- UCR rate gap (25 points): 40%+ gap = 25 pts, 30-39% = 20 pts, 20-29% = 15 pts
- 8-year lookback value (20 points): $500K+ = 20 pts, $250-500K = 15 pts, $100-250K = 10 pts
- Decision authority (15 points): Owner/partner = 15 pts, Administrator = 10 pts, Manager = 5 pts
- Competition (10 points): No current rep = 10 pts, Limited rep = 5 pts, Full rep = 0 pts

**Minimum qualifying score: 65 points**

### Handoff Criteria to SAI Recovery

**Qualified Lead Package Contents:**
- Complete discovery call notes and recording
- Financial impact analysis and recovery estimates
- Practice background and key stakeholder information
- Competitive landscape and urgency factors
- Recommended approach and next steps

**Handoff Requirements:**
- Minimum $100K total recovery potential
- Confirmed decision-maker interest
- Available documentation for 8-year lookback
- No disqualifying competition factors
- 60+ qualification score

**Success Metrics:**
- 40% discovery call to qualification rate
- 60% qualification to SAI Recovery close rate
- Average $200K recovery potential per qualified lead
- 45-day average cycle from first contact to signed agreement
