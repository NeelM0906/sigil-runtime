# ONBOARDING-WORKFLOW.md - WC Client Integration System

## Onboarding Philosophy

Successful WC recovery depends on seamless client integration, comprehensive data collection, and immediate case identification. The onboarding process must efficiently transition prospects from "qualified lead" to "active recovery client" while establishing the foundation for 8-year lookback analysis and ongoing case management.

## Pre-Onboarding (SAI Recovery Handoff)

### Qualified Lead Package Transfer

**Lead Package Contents:**
- Complete qualification scorecard with 65+ score
- Discovery call recording and detailed notes
- Financial impact analysis and recovery estimates
- Practice background and stakeholder mapping
- Competitive landscape assessment
- Recommended approach and urgency factors
- Next steps timeline and decision maker preferences

**Handoff Meeting (SAI BD-WC → SAI Recovery):**
- 30-minute transfer meeting within 24 hours
- Review qualification scores and key insights
- Discuss practice-specific approach recommendations
- Identify potential challenges or concerns
- Establish timeline and responsibility handoff
- Confirm first meeting scheduling

### Initial SAI Recovery Contact

**Contact Timeline:** Within 48 hours of handoff

**First Touch Strategy:**
```
"Dr. [Name], this is [SAI Recovery Rep] from Callagy Recovery. My colleague [SAI BD-WC] shared your interest in exploring Workers Compensation recovery opportunities. Based on our analysis of practices like yours, we typically identify $200K-500K in recoverable underpayments. I'd like to schedule a brief consultation to review your specific situation and explain our process. Would [specific time options] work for you?"
```

**Meeting Scheduling:**
- Offer 2-3 specific time slots within one week
- 60-minute initial consultation meeting
- Practice location preferred (in-person when possible)
- Decision makers and billing manager attendance
- Preparation materials sent in advance

## Phase 1: Initial Consultation (Week 1)

### Meeting Agenda (60 minutes)

**Opening (10 minutes):**
- Relationship establishment and trust building
- Review of practice background and WC volume
- Confirmation of qualified lead insights
- Meeting agenda and timeline setting

**UCR Rate Analysis Presentation (15 minutes):**
- Industry payment patterns for their specialty
- Specific UCR rate benchmarks for their procedures
- Estimated underpayment calculations
- 8-year lookback opportunity explanation

**Callagy Recovery Process Overview (15 minutes):**
- Contingent fee structure explanation
- Recovery methodology and legal approach
- Timeline expectations and process steps
- Success stories from similar practices

**Practice-Specific Assessment (15 minutes):**
- Current WC volume and payment patterns
- Historical billing data availability
- Documentation and records review
- Carrier-specific payment issues

**Next Steps and Decision Process (5 minutes):**
- Agreement terms and contingent fee structure
- Information requirements for formal analysis
- Timeline for decision and contract execution
- Follow-up meeting scheduling

### Required Deliverables

**Meeting Output:**
- Signed confidentiality agreement
- Practice assessment worksheet completion
- Historical data collection authorization
- Document access permissions
- Follow-up meeting scheduled

**Follow-up Materials (within 24 hours):**
- Written UCR rate analysis for their specialty
- Contingent fee agreement draft
- Historical data collection checklist
- Success case studies from similar practices
- Next steps timeline and contact information

## Phase 2: Historical Analysis (Weeks 2-3)

### Data Collection Process

**Billing Records Request (8-year lookback):**

**Required Documentation:**
- WC case lists with dates of service and payments
- Remittance advice from all WC carriers
- Fee schedules and contracted rates (if any)
- Denial notices and appeal documentation
- Billing software reports for WC claims
- Practice management system exports

**Data Collection Methods:**
- Secure file transfer portal access
- On-site data extraction (if preferred)
- Billing company coordination (if applicable)
- Electronic records system integration
- Physical file review (when necessary)

**Data Organization Framework:**
```
Client Data Structure:
- Year 1-8 folders
- Carrier-specific subfolders
- Procedure code categorization
- Payment vs. denial segregation
- Appeal documentation tracking
```

### Analysis Execution

**UCR Rate Comparison Process:**
- Procedure-by-procedure payment analysis
- Geographic UCR rate benchmarking
- Carrier-specific payment pattern review
- Denial reason classification
- Recovery opportunity quantification

**Historical Underpayment Calculation:**
- 8-year procedure volume assessment
- UCR rate gap identification by year
- Carrier-specific underpayment totals
- Interest and penalty calculations
- Total recovery opportunity summary

**Documentation Requirements:**
- Detailed analysis spreadsheets
- UCR rate research documentation
- Legal precedent research
- Carrier payment policy analysis
- Recovery strategy recommendations
